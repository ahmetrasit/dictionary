# Adjudication audit

## Scope and result

This audit compares the three adjudication tables only with `resonance_index.json` and the frozen cohort profiles. It does not propose additional candidates. The tables account for every indexed retained branch mention: **263/263** rows across **259** target records. The division is internally exact: water 85, sea 46, drink 46, and the remaining families 86. The zero-candidate families in `remaining.md` also agree with the index.

The global grades are **44 A, 75 B, 46 C, and 98 Reject**. Thus 119/263 candidates are supported at A/B and 144/263 are downgraded at C/Reject. The arithmetic is sound; the main audit issue is calibration. Several A grades produce a genuine interpretive change in the selected water lexeme, but others promote a contextual motif, a normal cohort function, or a neighboring word's action without showing why this target rather than an ordinary alternative carries the claimed effect.

## Most defensible A findings

These are the A-grade readings that most clearly explain why the selected lexeme matters to its scene. Paired targets in one ayah are treated as one interpretive finding, because they are not independent evidence.

1. **6:99, water / B006.** The water is not left at the generic rain-to-growth level. The command to inspect fruit specifically at ripening makes the earlier rain reappear as inward moisture, maturity, and visible freshness. Replacing the fruit sequence with generic vegetation would erase the reading, which is why this survives the cohort control.

2. **13:17, water / B005.** Water and heated metal produce corresponding foam, and the ayah itself distinguishes what vanishes from what benefits. The selected water therefore does more than fill valleys: it stages the separation of a persuasive surface from enduring substance. Without the metal-and-foam comparison, this would collapse back into ordinary torrent behavior.

3. **22:5, water / B004.** The same ayah first places reproductive fluid in wombs and then shows earth receiving water, swelling, and producing life. The rain lexeme is selected inside a deliberately paired generation sequence; ordinary wording about irrigation would weaken the human gestation/earth revival parallel.

4. **77:20, water / B003.** The reproductive fluid is immediately taken up by an anaphoric pronoun and placed in a secure receptacle until a measured term. The target is therefore read not only as biological substance but as content deliberately lodged, protected, and timed. Removing the placement construction would remove the secondary contribution.

5. **2:50, sea / B003.** The sea is acted upon as something parted, and the opening becomes the rescued group's route. It is selected as a boundary that can become passage; a generic statement that the people were rescued near water would lose that reversal.

6. **18:61, sea / B003.** The fish takes a path inside the sea described as a tunnel-like route. Here the sea is not a normal surface for ships but an interior capable of retaining a sign-bearing corridor. That concrete path distinguishes it from the cohort's recurrent navigation scenes.

7. **18:109, both sea targets / B002.** Turning the sea into ink converts physical amplitude into expressive supply, and exhausting it makes even maximal created breadth an inadequate measure for the divine words. A mere reference to a large sea would not perform this transfer from extent to writing capacity.

8. **20:77, sea / B003.** A dry road is made within the sea. The selected lexeme holds obstacle and escape route together, so replacing it with ordinary travel language would remove the scene's central transformation.

9. **26:63, sea / B003.** Strike, splitting, and separated masses form a complete local mechanism: the sea becomes cleft and the cleft becomes passage. This is stronger than a remote splitting motif because every step acts directly on the target.

10. **2:93, drink / B011.** The nonliteral drinking into hearts is set against commanded hearing, claimed hearing, and enacted disobedience. The verb explains a selective inward uptake: the calf is absorbed while the covenant is not understood. A neutral verb such as “liked” would lose the irony between bodily intake and failed comprehension.

11. **2:249, both drink targets / B002.** The permitted handful fixes the share, while the plural drinking marks the majority's crossing of that measure. The two target forms make reception itself the test: one remains within allotment and the other exposes excess. Generic river-crossing language would not classify the army through quantity consumed.

12. **16:69, drink / B007.** Varied color is in the liquid and healing appears in its recipients. The drink is selected as the carrier through which source qualities enter bodies; removing either the internal variation or bodily outcome would make the proposed permeation only thematic.

13. **23:33, both drink targets / B003.** The opponents use matched eating and drinking to assimilate the messenger to the audience. These verbs are selected as public markers of shared human membership, not as comments on beverage quality. The reciprocal forms on both sides make that classification unusually explicit.

14. **76:5, drink / B007.** Explicit mixture gives the cup an intentionally imbued composition, and drinking is the means by which that composition is received. An unmixed drink would preserve ordinary reward but eliminate the branch-specific interpretation.

15. **22:19, hot_water / B003.** The hot water is instrumentally linked to rendering what is inside bodies and their skins. It is not just painful liquid; the selected lexeme names the solvent that collapses the boundary between inner and outer bodily material.

16. **46:24, rain / B002.** The people select a rain-bearing label for the approaching cloud, and the immediate correction reveals an all-destroying wind. The target records their tragic misclassification of expected provision as punishment; a narrator's plain announcement of destruction would lose that irony.

17. **2:264, downpour / B002.** The heavy rain strips the thinly covered rock just as injury and ostentation nullify the spending. The water event is selected to expose the substrate and enact moral loss physically; lighter or merely productive rain would change the parable's result.

18. **28:23, water_arrival / B007.** Moses enters a water point already governed by crowd order, delayed turns, and subsequent departure. The arrival is consequently entry into an active succession whose disruption initiates the encounter. This is the stronger of the two A branches at this target because the local sequence, not only the existence of a share, changes the verb's force.

## A grades needing restraint

The following A grades look overstated under the reports' own tests of word-local attachment, cohort distinctiveness, and contribution beyond primary meaning.

- **2:74 water / B008:** the heart and cognition contrast is rhetorically strong, but the target is water issuing from responsive stone. “Dull heart-water” belongs to the contrasted hearts and is inferred by reversal; the water word itself never takes that property. **B or C** is more defensible.

- **10:24 and 24:39 water / B005:** both scenes concern deceptive appearance, but the indexed branch is specifically metallic coating. At 10:24 water enables adornment without itself coating it; at 24:39 the mirage is false presentation without a metal or plating mechanism. These are illuminating analogies, but the reports generalize the branch into deception at large. **B** is the prudent ceiling.

- **16:14 sea / B003:** ships cutting through the sea are part of the neutral cohort's ordinary navigation-and-provision pattern. The sea is traversed, not opened into an enduring cleft. The reading does not explain a scene change beyond normal seafaring, so **B or C** fits better.

- **18:63 sea / B003:** the corridor trigger belongs most directly to the preceding fish-path token. Recollection preserves the sign, but the A grade is less target-local than 18:61. **B** would preserve the echo without treating it as independent top-grade evidence.

- **82:3 sea / B003:** bursting open is the target's explicit end-time predicate. The water and drown reports otherwise reject a branch when it simply restates the stated event, even when the wording is exact. Unless “cleft” adds something beyond the primary bursting of the seas, this should be **B at most**.

- **2:93 drink / B008:** inward calf attachment is explicit, but covenant “taking/holding” does not put a rope around a neck or make the drinking verb a tether. The branch is a secondary metaphor laid over an already nonliteral target, so **B** is safer than A.

- **2:187 drink / B012:** the following wealth-consumption prohibition creates a sharp verbal contrast, but the target drinking remains licensed nourishment rather than wealth being consumed. The alternative prohibition illuminates the ethical frame more than it changes this target. **B** is sufficient.

- **2:259 drink / B011:** repeated looking and eventual knowing make the preserved drink evidence, but they do not turn preservation into understanding-as-drinking. The cognition attaches to the demonstration as a whole. This is **B or C**, not A.

- **36:73 drink / B012:** livestock are property and sources of food and drink, yet that is already the cohort profile's ordinary benefit inventory for this outlier. The A rationale largely restates owned provision; **B** better reflects the limited surprise.

- **69:24 drink / B012:** this is the clearest internal calibration problem. The cohort identifies 52:19, 69:24, and 77:43 as the same recurrent “eat and drink as recompense” cluster, while the report rejects the branch at the other two. Prior deeds and accounting do not by themselves explain why 69:24 alone becomes wealth consumed. It should be **C or Reject** absent a target-local difference.

- **76:21 water_give / B009:** colored cloth is adjacent, but the target verb administers a pure drink; it does not dye cloth or explicitly saturate the recipients. The parallel is attractive and may support **B**, but the inferred transfer cannot bear A. The same scene's drink/B007 is already graded B, which sharpens the inconsistency.

- **28:24 water_give / B002:** restored access is real, but the neutral `سقي` profile already treats Midian as human watering service exposing unequal access. The A rationale describes the primary social function of Moses' act rather than a second contribution. **B** is the appropriate grade.

- **79:1 drown / B004:** pulling plus an intensive limit resembles a fully drawn bow, but no bow, string, or projectile appears. Because the cohort expressly flags this as the lone non-water outlier, the missing mechanical object matters. The reading is plausible at **B**, not demonstrated at A.

- **56:42 hot_water / B001:** nearby same-root black smoke is a precise lexical cue, but it colors the surrounding anti-refreshment environment, not necessarily the hot water. **B** would recognize the atmospheric linkage without claiming that the target itself becomes black smoke.

- **27:58 rain / B001:** productive rain nearby gives the punitive shower a useful inverse, but punitive targeted descent is already the rain cohort's dominant pattern. The good-rain alternative clarifies evaluation without changing the selected target beyond bad rain. **B** is more consistent.

- **42:28 life_rain / B004:** release after despair and spreading mercy are explicit, but relief after hardship is already a primary frame of the `غيث` cohort and close to the selected lexeme's contextual force. The grade should be **B or Reject**, depending on whether the branch is treated as added meaning or ordinary entailment.

- **24:43 rain_drop / B004:** the danger to eyesight attaches most immediately to the lightning in the weather sequence, not to the rain target. For an expressly anomalous eye branch, participation in the same display is too weak to overcome that separation. **C** is warranted.

- **12:19 water_arrival / B006 and B007:** the bucket, not the target delegate, is the hanging length that reaches an endpoint; and being sent ahead is already the designated water-arriver's narrative role. The first is **C** for displaced attachment, while the vanguard reading is **B** as a useful but near-primary specification.

## Major negative controls

- **Ordinary hydrology stays ordinary.** Sky descent, entry into earth, revival, irrigation, plentiful floodwater, and direct drinking supply dominate the water cohort. The many water/B002 and water/B003 rejections correctly show that scale, divine agency, a beneficiary, or a productive result does not by itself make a secondary reading.

- **Normal travel does not become sea resonance.** Fourteen of sixteen sea/B009 proposals are rejected because ships, carriage, work, bounty, peril, and rescue already define the sea cohort. The stronger sea findings require the water body to become an opening, corridor, dry road, or transferred measure, not merely a route vessels use.

- **Literal consequences are not second meanings.** All thirteen drown/B002 proposals are rejected because causative drowning normally entails death. The same control should govern any A candidate whose branch only renames the target's explicit predicate, especially 82:3.

- **Cohort-normal boundaries do not automatically upgrade drink.** Shares, scheduled turns, permission, excess limits, reward drinks, and punitive anti-refreshment recur throughout the drink profile. Exact allotment at 2:249 changes character and group outcome; generic recompense at 69:24 does not clear the same bar.

- **Negation belongs to the target actually negated.** Pleasant, pure, harmless drinks at 16:66 and 37:46 do not activate unpleasant drinkability, and the bad water at 47:15 does not transfer to the contrasted Paradise drinkers. Likewise, restraints or cognition elsewhere in a window cannot be assigned to a drink target merely because they create thematic contrast.

- **Remote and source-separated routes remain high burden.** Six of seven `يم`/aim crossings stay C or Reject because intention belongs to commands, pursuits, promises, or casting verbs, not to the water noun. Generic travel does not activate rain's land-crossing branch; an unnamed tree does not activate the torrent homonym; and generic companions do not fully establish the intimate `حميم` route.

- **Branch-specific material matters.** Fruit lists without ripening cues do not establish water/B006; salt directly predicated of a sea is primary quality; generic darkness does not make hot water black; and a visual hazard caused by lightning should not be reassigned to rain. These controls are the best protection against turning a nearby keyword into a catalog match.

- **Explicit-none families are useful controls, not missing results.** Wave, fresh_sweet, stale_water, well, split_emergence, and pouring have no frozen candidate rows. Their zero counts agree with the index and demonstrate that cohort membership alone was not treated as evidence of a secondary activation.

## Bottom line

The adjudication is mechanically complete and mostly conservative at B/C/Reject, especially for ordinary rain, supply, sea travel, lethal drowning, and remote homonyms. Its best A findings select water language that materially reorganizes the scene: rain becomes ripeness or generation, sea becomes passage or expressive measure, drinking becomes measured reception or inward uptake, and punitive weather becomes tragic misrecognition. The weaker A findings instead describe what the scene already does, borrow their trigger from an adjacent object, or broaden a narrow branch into a general theme. Those should be downgraded before the A set is used as a high-confidence interpretive layer.
