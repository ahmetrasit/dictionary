# batch_015 Water Secondary Resonance

## 8:54 — كَدَأْبِ ءَالِ فِرْعَوْنَ وَٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِمْ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَٰتِ رَبِّهِمْ فَأَهْلَكْنَٰهُم بِذُنُوبِهِمْ وَأَغْرَقْنَآ ءَالَ فِرْعَوْنَ وَكُلٌّ كَانُوا۟ ظَٰلِمِينَ

### أَغْرَقْ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The verb states that God caused Pharaoh's house to drown; it supplies the concrete watery mode of their destruction while leaving the preceding statement of destruction intact.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The doubled comparison with earlier rejecters (8:52, 8:54), the intervening account of a favor changing only after inward change (8:53), and the repeated covenant-breaking of 8:56 make the drowning one instance of a recurring moral pattern rather than an isolated disaster.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B002` is retained: beside drowning as the mode, the verb participates in the ayah's broader language of killing or destruction. `root_001080/B005` is also retained: the result is presented as exhaustive, absorbing the whole implicated collective.

**Activation trace.** For B002, the immediately coordinated `فَأَهْلَكْنَٰهُم ... وَأَغْرَقْنَا` explicitly pairs destruction with drowning; the bridge is that the second action specifies and intensifies the first, so the target contributes both submersion and lethal eradication. For B005, `وَكُلٌّ كَانُوا۟ ظَٰلِمِينَ`, reinforced by `كُلِّ مَرَّةٍ` in 8:56, makes totality conspicuous; the bridge from the branch's whole-scope absorption is that no part of the culpable pattern escapes, so the drowning reads as comprehensive judgment on Pharaoh's house.

**Negative evidence.** No tear-filled eye or saturated land activates B003, no bow or extreme draw activates B004, no draught activates B006, and no ornamented bridle activates B008.

## 10:73 — فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَنَجَّيْنَٰهُ وَمَن مَّعَهُۥ فِى ٱلْفُلْكِ وَجَعَلْنَٰهُمْ خَلَٰٓئِفَ وَأَغْرَقْنَا ٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَا فَٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَٰقِبَةُ ٱلْمُنذَرِينَ

### أَغْرَقْ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The verb says that God drowned those who denied the signs, in contrast to saving Noah and his companions in the ark.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The drowning is framed by a transfer of historical position: those aboard are not merely rescued but made successors, while the reader is ordered to inspect the deniers' outcome. The water event thus marks both removal and succession.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `فَنَجَّيْنَٰهُ ... فِى ٱلْفُلْكِ` supplies a sharp rescue/drowning contrast, and `جَعَلْنَٰهُمْ خَلَٰٓئِفَ` supplies the succession dimension, but these clarify the primary event rather than activate a distinct غ ر ق branch.

**Negative evidence.** The lethal consequence of drowning and the command `فَأَجْمِعُوا۟ أَمْرَكُمْ` are insufficient by themselves for B002 or B005: the former adds no independent killing cue, and the latter concerns assembling a plan rather than whole-scope immersion. There is no local trigger for B003, B004, B006, or B008.

## 11:37 — وَٱصْنَعِ ٱلْفُلْكَ بِأَعْيُنِنَا وَوَحْيِنَا وَلَا تُخَٰطِبْنِى فِى ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوٓا۟ إِنَّهُم مُّغْرَقُونَ

### مُّغْرَقُونَ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The passive participle declares the wrongdoers destined to be drowned, explaining why Noah must build the ark and must not plead concerning them.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window juxtaposes divinely supervised making (`بِأَعْيُنِنَا وَوَحْيِنَا`) with the people's mockery of that making in 11:38. The drowning is therefore already certain while its visible means is still being ridiculed.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The ark, the prohibition on intercession, and the ensuing `عَذَابٌ` make the primary drowning a fixed punitive outcome, but none supplies an independent image belonging to another غ ر ق branch.

**Negative evidence.** `عَذَابٌ` does not independently establish B002's broader killing sense, and `كُلَّمَا` is distributive timing rather than B005's whole-field absorption. The window has no tears or saturated land (B003), bow-draw (B004), draught (B006), or ornamented bridle (B008).

## 17:69 — أَمْ أَمِنتُمْ أَن يُعِيدَكُمْ فِيهِ تَارَةً أُخْرَىٰ فَيُرْسِلَ عَلَيْكُمْ قَاصِفًا مِّنَ ٱلرِّيحِ فَيُغْرِقَكُم بِمَا كَفَرْتُمْ ثُمَّ لَا تَجِدُوا۟ لَكُمْ عَلَيْنَا بِهِۦ تَبِيعًا

### يُغْرِقَ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The verb presents a possible divine causing-to-drown at sea through a violent wind, in consequence of disbelief.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Rescue from sea to land in 17:67 is followed by insecurity even on land in 17:68 and the possibility of being returned to sea in 17:69. Against 17:70's honoring and carrying of Adam's children on land and sea, the same domains of transport and favor remain domains of accountability.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `ٱلْبَحْرِ`, `قَاصِفًا مِّنَ ٱلرِّيحِ`, and the prior `نَجَّىٰكُمْ` visibly anchor literal drowning and its reversal; `لَا تَجِدُوا۟ ... تَبِيعًا` adds the absence of recourse, not a second غ ر ق sense.

**Negative evidence.** Neither the danger's lethality nor the storm independently triggers B002; no expression of exhaustive absorption supports B005. B003, B004, B006, and B008 lack their required local images.

## 17:103 — فَأَرَادَ أَن يَسْتَفِزَّهُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَرْضِ فَأَغْرَقْنَٰهُ وَمَن مَّعَهُۥ جَمِيعًا

### أَغْرَقْ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The verb reports that God drowned Pharaoh and those with him after Pharaoh sought to dislodge the Israelites from the land.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Pharaoh's intended removal from the land is reversed: he and his company are removed, while the Israelites are told in 17:104 to dwell in that same land. Drowning becomes the hinge of a territorial reversal.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B005` is retained: the drowning takes in the whole opposing company and clears the field they had tried to control.

**Activation trace.** `وَمَن مَّعَهُۥ جَمِيعًا` explicitly marks total inclusion, while `يَسْتَفِزَّهُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَرْضِ` and `ٱسْكُنُوا۟ ٱلْأَرْضَ` frame a contested field before and after the event. The abductive bridge to B005's whole-scope occupation is the exhaustive removal of Pharaoh's company from that field; the target consequently contributes comprehensive reversal, not merely individual submersion.

**Negative evidence.** No independent killing verb warrants B002. The window contains no tear or saturation cue for B003, bow for B004, draught for B006, or bridle ornament for B008.

## 18:71 — فَٱنطَلَقَا حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا رَكِبَا فِى ٱلسَّفِينَةِ خَرَقَهَا قَالَ أَخَرَقْتَهَا لِتُغْرِقَ أَهْلَهَا لَقَدْ جِئْتَ شَيْـًٔا إِمْرًا

### تُغْرِقَ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** In Moses' question, the verb construes the hole in the ship as an action liable or intended to cause its people to drown.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The target occurs inside a mistaken inference: a visible breach is immediately read as a monstrous purpose, although the surrounding instructions stress suspended questioning, patience, and awaiting an explanation.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B002` is retained: Moses hears the prospective drowning as the killing of the ship's people.

**Activation trace.** The purposive construction `خَرَقْتَهَا لِتُغْرِقَ أَهْلَهَا` assigns the threatened result to an agent, and `لَقَدْ جِئْتَ شَيْـًٔا إِمْرًا` registers moral horror at the deed. The bridge to B002 is the accusation's construal of breaching the vessel as deliberate lethal action; this makes the target contribute perceived homicide by drowning, while its primary nautical sense remains.

**Negative evidence.** Because the drowning is only Moses' inferred purpose, the text does not license further extension beyond B002. Nothing activates B003, B004, B005, B006, or B008.

## 21:77 — وَنَصَرْنَٰهُ مِنَ ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَآ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ قَوْمَ سَوْءٍ فَأَغْرَقْنَٰهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ

### أَغْرَقْ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The verb reports God's drowning of the people who denied the signs, as the counterpart to saving and supporting Noah and his family.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding verse calls Noah's ordeal `ٱلْكَرْبِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ`, so drowning is presented not only as the deniers' end but as the complete release of Noah's household from an immense distress.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B005` is retained: the drowning encompasses the opposing people without remainder.

**Activation trace.** The direct modifier `أَجْمَعِينَ` is an explicit totality trigger. The abductive bridge to B005's whole-scope absorption is that the water-event takes the entire culpable group; the target thus marks exhaustive communal removal and the completeness of Noah's deliverance.

**Negative evidence.** No separate killing expression activates B002. The nearby field and flock belong to the next judicial episode and do not establish watery saturation for B003; B004, B006, and B008 likewise have no local trigger.

## 23:27 — فَأَوْحَيْنَآ إِلَيْهِ أَنِ ٱصْنَعِ ٱلْفُلْكَ بِأَعْيُنِنَا وَوَحْيِنَا فَإِذَا جَآءَ أَمْرُنَا وَفَارَ ٱلتَّنُّورُ فَٱسْلُكْ فِيهَا مِن كُلٍّ زَوْجَيْنِ ٱثْنَيْنِ وَأَهْلَكَ إِلَّا مَن سَبَقَ عَلَيْهِ ٱلْقَوْلُ مِنْهُمْ وَلَا تُخَٰطِبْنِى فِى ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوٓا۟ إِنَّهُم مُّغْرَقُونَ

### مُّغْرَقُونَ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The participle declares the wrongdoers destined for drowning and grounds the instruction not to address God on their behalf.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The same command carefully specifies preservation inside the ark, down to pairs and family exceptions, while declaring another class already doomed; 23:28-29 then turns survival into praise and a request for blessed landing.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `فَارَ ٱلتَّنُّورُ`, the ark, embarkation, and the following rescue visibly build the literal flood sequence. `سَبَقَ عَلَيْهِ ٱلْقَوْلُ` adds decreed certainty, but does not independently activate another غ ر ق branch.

**Negative evidence.** `مِن كُلٍّ زَوْجَيْنِ` concerns complete provisioning of the ark, not whole-scope absorption of those drowned, so B005 is not retained. The target's fatal implication alone does not establish B002, and B003, B004, B006, and B008 have no matching cue.

## 25:37 — وَقَوْمَ نُوحٍ لَّمَّا كَذَّبُوا۟ ٱلرُّسُلَ أَغْرَقْنَٰهُمْ وَجَعَلْنَٰهُمْ لِلنَّاسِ ءَايَةً وَأَعْتَدْنَا لِلظَّٰلِمِينَ عَذَابًا أَلِيمًا

### أَغْرَقْ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The verb says that Noah's people were drowned after denying the messengers; their fate is then made a sign for humanity.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The people become communicative after their destruction: their drowning is made `ءَايَةً`, then placed among many peoples and examples in 25:38-39. Their end is not only punitive but exemplary.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B002` is retained: the drowning participates in the window's generalized register of annihilating peoples.

**Activation trace.** The immediately preceding `فَدَمَّرْنَٰهُمْ تَدْمِيرًا` and the following `وَكُلًّا تَبَّرْنَا تَتْبِيرًا` flank the target with emphatic destruction formulas. The abductive bridge to B002's extended use for killing is this coordinated series of modes of destroying peoples; the target contributes both the particular water-death of Noah's people and their place in a broader catalogue of eradication.

**Negative evidence.** The repeated `كُلًّا` distributes treatment across named peoples but does not depict B005's absorption of the drowning event's whole field. No trigger supports B003, B004, B006, or B008.

## 26:66 — ثُمَّ أَغْرَقْنَا ٱلْءَاخَرِينَ

### أَغْرَقْ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The verb states that, after Moses and his companions were saved, God drowned the others.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The extreme brevity makes sequence and partition decisive: the others are first brought near, Moses and everyone with him are saved, and only then are the others drowned; the event is immediately identified as a sign and followed by the pairing of divine might and mercy.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B005` is retained: the contrasting group labels make the drowning exhaustive of the remaining side.

**Activation trace.** `وَمَن مَّعَهُۥٓ أَجْمَعِينَ` explicitly totalizes the saved company, while `ٱلْءَاخَرِينَ` designates the complementary remainder. The bridge to B005's whole-scope absorption is the categorical partition: once the entire first group is rescued, drowning takes the whole other group. The target therefore contributes completeness of separation as well as submersion.

**Negative evidence.** There is no independent killing lexeme for B002 and no local image for tear/land saturation (B003), bow-drawing (B004), a draught (B006), or an ornamented bridle (B008).
