# batch_020 Water Secondary Resonance

## 34:16 — فَأَعْرَضُوا۟ فَأَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ سَيْلَ ٱلْعَرِمِ وَبَدَّلْنَٰهُم بِجَنَّتَيْهِمْ جَنَّتَيْنِ ذَوَاتَىْ أُكُلٍ خَمْطٍ وَأَثْلٍ وَشَىْءٍ مِّن سِدْرٍ قَلِيلٍ

### سَيْلَ — س ي ل

**Primary reading.** The noun denotes the destructive torrent sent against the people after their turning away, the event that effects the loss of their former gardens.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window moves from two flourishing gardens on the right and left (34:15) to two gardens of altered, scant produce, and then names this reversal recompense (34:17). The torrent therefore exposes the contingency of an apparently secure, spatially ordered prosperity.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000770/B004` is retained as an arboreal afterimage; it supplements rather than replaces the flowing-torrent sense.

**Activation trace.** The immediate continuation `جَنَّتَيْنِ ذَوَاتَىْ أُكُلٍ خَمْطٍ وَأَثْلٍ وَشَىْءٍ مِّن سِدْرٍ قَلِيلٍ` visibly inventories trees and woody produce, activating B004's *sayal*-tree branch. Because the torrent is the agent in the same sentence's exchange of one pair of gardens for another, the branch bridges the flowing cause to the arboreal condition left in its wake. `سَيْلَ` thus contributes not only inundation but a transition into a diminished tree-world.

**Negative evidence.** The spreading villages and travel of 34:18 do not depict extension *like a flow* closely enough for B002. The right/left gardens are not a man's beard-sides or flanks, so B005 is not retained. No iron tang or specifically *sayal*-named tree activates B003 beyond its high burden or strengthens B004 into literal tree identification; B004 remains only resonance.

## 2:264 — يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُبْطِلُوا۟ صَدَقَٰتِكُم بِٱلْمَنِّ وَٱلْأَذَىٰ كَٱلَّذِى يُنفِقُ مَالَهُۥ رِئَآءَ ٱلنَّاسِ وَلَا يُؤْمِنُ بِٱللَّهِ وَٱلْيَوْمِ ٱلْءَاخِرِ فَمَثَلُهُۥ كَمَثَلِ صَفْوَانٍ عَلَيْهِ تُرَابٌ فَأَصَابَهُۥ وَابِلٌ فَتَرَكَهُۥ صَلْدًا لَّا يَقْدِرُونَ عَلَىٰ شَىْءٍ مِّمَّا كَسَبُوا۟ وَٱللَّهُ لَا يَهْدِى ٱلْقَوْمَ ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ

### وَابِلٌ — و ب ل

**Primary reading.** The word is a heavy downpour that washes the thin soil from smooth rock and leaves it bare, imaging the nullification of ostentatious charity.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The very same heavy rain makes the sincere garden yield doubly in 2:265. The window therefore makes the downpour diagnostic rather than intrinsically destructive: its result discloses what lies beneath the visible covering.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001619/B002` is retained. Its role is the harmful weight and consequence enacted through the literal rain.

**Activation trace.** The commands and results `لَا تُبْطِلُوا۟`, `بِٱلْمَنِّ وَٱلْأَذَىٰ`, `فَتَرَكَهُۥ صَلْدًا`, and `لَّا يَقْدِرُونَ عَلَىٰ شَىْءٍ مِّمَّا كَسَبُوا۟` visibly supply injury, nullification, and a feared outcome, activating B002's severity/harmful-consequence branch. The abductive bridge is that the physical weight of the downpour realizes the moral *وبال* of display and injury. `وَابِلٌ` consequently measures the crushing consequence of the act, not merely rainfall intensity.

**Negative evidence.** The bare rock's failure to retain soil is not bodily unwholesomeness or unpalatability, so B003 is not retained here. No thick implement, bundle, or animal-mating cue supports B004 or the single-source B006.

## 2:265 — وَمَثَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَٰلَهُمُ ٱبْتِغَآءَ مَرْضَاتِ ٱللَّهِ وَتَثْبِيتًا مِّنْ أَنفُسِهِمْ كَمَثَلِ جَنَّةٍۭ بِرَبْوَةٍ أَصَابَهَا وَابِلٌ فَـَٔاتَتْ أُكُلَهَا ضِعْفَيْنِ فَإِن لَّمْ يُصِبْهَا وَابِلٌ فَطَلٌّ وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ

### وَابِلٌ (2:265:15) — و ب ل

**Primary reading.** The first occurrence is the heavy rain that strikes a well-sited garden and leads it to give twice its produce, imaging sincere expenditure made from inward firmness.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding rock is stripped by a downpour, whereas this garden is multiplied by one; the following ruined garden (2:266) shows that abundance itself can remain vulnerable. The beneficial result rests on grounded receptivity, not on the force being mild.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001619/B002` and `root_001619/B003` are retained. B002 supplies contrastive weight or ordeal; B003 supplies a qualitative, assimilability counterimage.

**Activation trace.** For `root_001619/B002`, the preceding `وَابِلٌ فَتَرَكَهُۥ صَلْدًا` and the following `إِعْصَارٌ فِيهِ نَارٌ فَٱحْتَرَقَتْ` visibly display heavy impacts with harmful outcomes. The same force instead elicits doubled fruit here, so its role is a weighty test whose result reveals the garden's grounding; `وَابِلٌ` contributes proof of firmness, not merely irrigation. For `root_001619/B003`, the direct result `أُكُلَهَا` and the 2:267 opposition `مِن طَيِّبَاتِ مَا كَسَبْتُمْ` / `ٱلْخَبِيثَ ... وَلَسْتُم بِـَٔاخِذِيهِ` activate the branch's food and acceptability domain. Set against B003's unwholesomeness, the garden's assimilated rain and doubled food make the target mark an input transformed into acceptable, wholesome yield.

**Negative evidence.** No carried club, beating implement, bundle, or animal-state expression activates B004 or the single-source B006.

### وَابِلٌ (2:265:22) — و ب ل

**Primary reading.** The second occurrence repeats heavy rain inside a negated condition: even if such rain does not reach the garden, lighter moisture suffices.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The conditional reduces dependence on spectacular intensity. In the window's comparison of stripped rock, fruitful garden, and burned garden, enduring fruitfulness is presented as a property of the recipient rather than a guarantee supplied by dramatic weather.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001619/B002` is retained as a negated threshold of weight and severity.

**Activation trace.** The visible contrast `فَإِن لَّمْ يُصِبْهَا وَابِلٌ فَطَلٌّ` places the second target against the lighter `طَلٌّ`, while 2:264 has just shown a `وَابِلٌ` producing the harmful result `فَتَرَكَهُۥ صَلْدًا`. These cues activate `root_001619/B002`: the heavy incidence functions as the strong test that may be absent. The bridge is contrastive, not a claim that this rain harms the garden; `وَابِلٌ` adds that sincere fertility does not require the severest pressure to become effective.

**Negative evidence.** The food-quality cues attach directly to the first rain's produced `أُكُلَهَا`; they do not independently localize B003 in this negated second token. B004 and B006 lack concrete implement, bundle, or animal triggers.

## 24:43 — أَلَمْ تَرَ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُزْجِى سَحَابًا ثُمَّ يُؤَلِّفُ بَيْنَهُۥ ثُمَّ يَجْعَلُهُۥ رُكَامًا فَتَرَى ٱلْوَدْقَ يَخْرُجُ مِنْ خِلَٰلِهِۦ وَيُنَزِّلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مِن جِبَالٍ فِيهَا مِنۢ بَرَدٍ فَيُصِيبُ بِهِۦ مَن يَشَآءُ وَيَصْرِفُهُۥ عَن مَّن يَشَآءُ يَكَادُ سَنَا بَرْقِهِۦ يَذْهَبُ بِٱلْأَبْصَٰرِ

### وَدْقَ — و د ق

**Primary reading.** The word denotes descending rain seen emerging through the accumulated cloud.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window passes from universal worship and divine ownership to a staged cloud process, threatened sight, alternation of night and day, and creatures made from water. Rain is thus one visible movement inside a larger order of perception, life, and return.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001636/B001` and `root_001636/B004` are retained. B001 supplies directed coming; B004 supplies an eye-centered danger at the event's edge.

**Activation trace.** For `root_001636/B001`, `وَإِلَى ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمَصِيرُ` is followed by the ordered motion `يُزْجِى ... يُؤَلِّفُ ... يَجْعَلُهُۥ ... يَخْرُجُ`, visibly activating coming, approach, and arrival. The branch's role is kinetic: the `وَدْقَ` is not static material but an arrival brought through a sequence to its recipients. For anomalous `root_001636/B004`, the unusually exact trigger `يَكَادُ سَنَا بَرْقِهِۦ يَذْهَبُ بِٱلْأَبْصَٰرِ`, reinforced by `لِأُو۟لِى ٱلْأَبْصَٰرِ` in 24:44, meets the higher burden of the eye-disease branch. Its role is a perceptual counterimage: the rain-bearing display that invites seeing also nearly removes sight, so `وَدْقَ` carries a locally dangerous visual edge alongside nourishment.

**Negative evidence.** The animals made from water in 24:45 do not display mating desire, and walking on the belly is not navel flow; B002 and the single-source B009 fail. Hail and lightning do not supply the fixed severe-calamity idiom, a heat scene, a battle-ground, or a sharp sword, so B005-B008 are not retained.

## 30:48 — ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِى يُرْسِلُ ٱلرِّيَٰحَ فَتُثِيرُ سَحَابًا فَيَبْسُطُهُۥ فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ كَيْفَ يَشَآءُ وَيَجْعَلُهُۥ كِسَفًا فَتَرَى ٱلْوَدْقَ يَخْرُجُ مِنْ خِلَٰلِهِۦ فَإِذَآ أَصَابَ بِهِۦ مَن يَشَآءُ مِنْ عِبَادِهِۦٓ إِذَا هُمْ يَسْتَبْشِرُونَ

### وَدْقَ — و د ق

**Primary reading.** The word is rain emerging through pieces of cloud and reaching chosen servants, at which they rejoice.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding ayah sends messengers with clear signs, and the following ayat call rain an effect of mercy that revives dead earth and points to resurrection. The weather sequence therefore functions as a delivered sign as well as relief.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001636/B001` is retained. It gives the rain the role of an arriving, directed bearer.

**Activation trace.** The local sequence `أَرْسَلْنَا ... رُسُلًا ... فَجَآءُوهُم بِٱلْبَيِّنَٰتِ` (30:47) is answered by `يُرْسِلُ ٱلرِّيَٰحَ` and `أَصَابَ بِهِۦ مَن يَشَآءُ` (30:48). Sending, coming, and reaching visibly activate `root_001636/B001`, whose scope is approach and arrival. The abductive bridge is the parallel sent-and-arriving sequence: `وَدْقَ` contributes an arrival carrying mercy and a sign, not precipitation alone.

**Negative evidence.** `فَتَرَى` and `فَٱنظُرْ` concern seeing, not eye disease, so B004 does not pass. Vengeance in 30:47 lacks the fixed B005 expression; no mating, heat, battle-ground, sharp implement, or navel-flow cue supports B002 or B005-B009.

## 12:19 — وَجَآءَتْ سَيَّارَةٌ فَأَرْسَلُوا۟ وَارِدَهُمْ فَأَدْلَىٰ دَلْوَهُۥ قَالَ يَٰبُشْرَىٰ هَٰذَا غُلَٰمٌ وَأَسَرُّوهُ بِضَٰعَةً وَٱللَّهُ عَلِيمٌۢ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ

### وَارِدَ — و ر د

**Primary reading.** The word names the caravan's water-arriver or water-drawer, sent ahead to reach the well and obtain water.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The expected water task retrieves a boy, who is immediately concealed as merchandise and then sold before being received into a household. Ordinary arrival at a resource becomes the hinge of a person's passage between radically different statuses.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001640/B006` and `root_001640/B007` are retained. B006 supplies the downward reaching action; B007 supplies advance entry on behalf of the group.

**Activation trace.** For `root_001640/B006`, `فَأَدْلَىٰ دَلْوَهُۥ` is an exact visible image of something lengthened or let down until it reaches its destination. The branch makes the target's arrival recursive: the water-arriver reaches the well by making the bucket reach downward, and that reaching unexpectedly contacts the boy. For `root_001640/B007`, `جَآءَتْ سَيَّارَةٌ فَأَرْسَلُوا۟ وَارِدَهُمْ` shows the group arriving and dispatching one member ahead, directly activating advance and entry before others. The target thus contributes not only an occupational label but the vanguard action that opens the narrative's next sequence.

**Negative evidence.** `بِضَٰعَةً` and `دَرَٰهِمَ مَعْدُودَةٍ` concern merchandise and price, not a recurring allotted portion, so B002 is not retained. The previous `دَمٍ كَذِبٍ` does not explicitly state rose-red color or a vein, and there is no fever cue; B003-B005 fail.

## 22:45 — فَكَأَيِّن مِّن قَرْيَةٍ أَهْلَكْنَٰهَا وَهِىَ ظَالِمَةٌ فَهِىَ خَاوِيَةٌ عَلَىٰ عُرُوشِهَا وَبِئْرٍ مُّعَطَّلَةٍ وَقَصْرٍ مَّشِيدٍ

### بِئْرٍ — ب ء ر

**Primary reading.** The noun is a physical well left unused amid the remains of a destroyed wrongdoing settlement.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The disabled well and raised palace are paired after the collapsed dwellings: low life-sustaining infrastructure and high monumental construction alike remain as unusable evidence. The following appeal to traveling hearts and ears turns the abandoned landscape into material for perception.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The physical pit image belongs to the target's primary `root_000078/B001` branch. Although `أَخَذْتُهُمْ`, `أَهْلَكْنَٰهَا`, and the coming `ٱلْعَذَابِ` create a setting of calamity, no local speech, slander, or agent who causes another to fall into trouble supplies the mechanism required for `root_000078/B003`.

**Negative evidence.** Nothing is hidden, stored, or described as a reserve, so `root_000078/B002` does not pass. General destruction alone is insufficient to activate B003's specific figurative informer or slanderer.
