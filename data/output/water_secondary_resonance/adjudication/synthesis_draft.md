# Consolidated water secondary-resonance synthesis

## Interpretive synthesis

The water vocabulary does not divide primarily into synonyms for the same substance. Each family selects a different viewpoint on water: material in circulation, traversable expanse, enclosing destination, moving obstruction, timed descent, bodily uptake, social transfer, or terminal overwhelm. The strongest secondary branches succeed when they sharpen that established viewpoint in a locally exact way. They fail when they merely import an associated idea such as growth, provision, death, heat, or travel that the cohort already expresses directly.

### Substance, expanse, destination, and enclosure

**ماء** presents water most often as **material under control**. Its dominant movement is from sky to earth or interior and then into growth, purification, storage, drinking, or renewed access. This is why ordinary descent, abundance, irrigation, flood behavior, and provision produced so many rejections: those are already the noun's primary work. Its strongest secondary readings instead change what the material is seen to do. At 13:17, water carries foam beside heated metal, ornament, and explicit truth/falsehood, so it sorts false surface from enduring substance. At 22:5, the same-ayah sequence from **نطفة** and womb placement to rain-revived earth lets rain echo deposited generative fluid. At 24:39, apparent water is the mirage's satisfying but empty presentation. At 77:20, reproductive fluid becomes deliberately emplaced, protected, and timed content. Even 2:74's surprising dull-heart branch fits this material viewpoint contrastively: responsive stone releases its inner water while informed hearts do not yield an intelligent response.

The recurrent B readings preserve the same orientation. Water becomes inward ripening or transient freshness when fruit, color, maturity, or collapse makes that result visible; it becomes administered material when it is routed into earth, stored, allotted, imposed, or restored after withdrawal. These are weaker where one step remains between external water and inward moisture, or where supply and placement are already close to the primary construction.

**بحر**, by contrast, views water as **expanse, boundary, and medium of passage**. Ships, work, bounty, peril, and rescue are therefore ordinary sea behavior, not secondary travel branches. The strong cleft readings occur when the expanse itself is reconfigured: direct parting and dry-way language in the Exodus passages, or **سبيله ... سربا** at 18:61 and 18:63, turn the sea into an opened corridor. At 82:3, **فجرت** similarly makes the seas boundary-breaking clefts. The breadth branch is strongest not where the sea is merely large, but where its extent is transferred into another measure: at 18:109 and 31:27, sea-as-ink converts physical amplitude into expressive supply and then makes that supply fail before the divine words.

This explains why neighboring water-body words are not interchangeable. **يم** is not a navigable expanse but an **encompassing destination into which something is deliberately consigned** in the Moses-Pharaoh story world. Commands, promises, pursuit, or casting verbs normally carry the intention, so six of seven deliberate-aim proposals are C or Reject. Only the second token at 20:39 reaches B, because the **يم** itself is assigned conveyance of the casket to shore. **موج** selects neither substance nor destination but **water in enclosing, obscuring, or interposing motion**. Its primary cohort already covers surrounding peril, separation, layered darkness, and turbulent crowds; no secondary branch was retained because that moving-enclosure viewpoint explains the attested cases without supplementation.

### Different kinds of descent and flow

The rain terms distinguish the event by incidence, timing, visibility, force, or terrain effect. **مطر** foregrounds **targeted descent onto a recipient**, and its Quranic cohort is predominantly punitive. Ordinary punitive rain is therefore not secondary. The strongest readings arise through reversal: at 46:24 the people's desired-rain label is immediately corrected to an all-destroying wind, while at 27:58 bad rain is set against nearby garden-producing sky-water and becomes specifically anti-productive. B cases at 8:32 similarly preserve the falling schema while replacing sought water with stones or turning a rain request into an inverted request for punishment.

**غيث** instead foregrounds **timely relief and provision outside human control**. At 42:28, rain follows explicit despair and is coordinated with spreading mercy, so the relief branch is not a generic theme but the event's humanly experienced reversal. At 12:49 the drought-to-production sequence supports the same pattern at B because rain itself remains implicit. **ودق** selects **rain visibly emerging through configured cloud**. That visual viewpoint makes the high-burden branch at 24:43 possible: repeated sight language and the immediate threat to eyesight turn the rain-bearing display into a paradox of seeing and near loss of sight. The parallel sent-messenger and sent-rain sequence at 30:48 supports an arriving bearer of sign and mercy only at B.

**وابل** views rain through **force applied to a receiving substrate**. The paired spending parables show that identical heavy rain strips thin soil from rock but multiplies a rooted garden's yield. At 2:264, injury, nullification, bare rock, and lost earnings let physical severity enact moral harmful weight; the adjacent garden serves as a control showing that the downpour discloses the recipient rather than carrying one fixed moral value. **سيل** shifts attention to **terrain-bound flow and what it sorts or replaces**, while **ثجاج** directly names profuse pouring. Thus the truth/falsehood sorting at 13:17 can support a secondary coating branch on **ماء**, but the torrent's movement and the profuse rain's descent-to-growth chain remain primary descriptions. No branch was retained for **ثجاج**.

### Water qualities

Quality words impose a bodily or evaluative result rather than a new water location. **حميم** is **administered anti-refreshment**: it enters the body, is poured over it, or surrounds it in punitive space. General hell heat therefore does not activate another branch. At 22:19, **يصهر به** states the liquid's exact rendering action on interior and skin, making it an active solvent rather than merely hot water. At 56:42, immediate same-root **يحموم** joins it to black smoke and false shade, giving the water a locally exact dark atmospheric register. Intimate-helper and due-time echoes remain B because their bridges are homonymic or cross-root.

**أجاج** foregrounds **impaired drinkability**, **فرات** the **potable side of differentiated provision**, and **آسن** deterioration, present only under negation in preserved Garden water. Kindled fire supports a B-grade burning harshness for **أجاج** at 56:70, but stated saltiness remains primary. No branch was retained for **فرات** or the singleton **آسن**; their direct quality contrasts already do the interpretive work. The abandoned **بئر** likewise presents unused vertical access amid ruin, while **انبجاس** presents one source breaking into twelve socially mapped outflows. Both remain informative controls without frozen secondary candidates.

### Uptake, transfer, arrival, and overwhelm

**شرب** views water or another liquid from the recipient side as **uptake bounded by quality, quantity, time, or group entitlement**. This is why measured-share and social-organization branches recur at B: they deepen a stable cohort contour rather than unexpectedly redirect it. The strongest cases make uptake disclose something more exact. At 2:249, the permitted handful makes drinking a test of measured reception and the plural act a violation of allotment. At 16:69, varied color in the liquid and healing in recipients give a direct transmission of qualities. At 23:33, matched eating and drinking establish shared human identity. At 36:73, livestock as owned property converted into riding, food, and drinks realizes wealth consumed as provision.

The nonliteral outlier at 2:93 reveals the family's broader logic most sharply. Calf attachment plus taking and holding cues make inward absorption tethering, while the hearing/disobedience sequence contrasts content rejected cognitively with the calf successfully taken inward. The result is not a general metaphor of drinking but two exact A-grade developments of uptake. By comparison, hostile liquids in punishment support a recurrent B pattern of aversive but compelled intake; their frequency and explicit unpleasantness keep most below A.

**سقي** reverses the perspective: it foregrounds **transfer from provider or source to recipient**. Ordinary divine supply, irrigation, milk-giving, and afterlife administration are therefore primary. Secondary force appears when transfer changes access or saturation. At 28:24, Moses' act removes the women's explicit barrier at a congested communal source, making watering socially enabling. At 76:21, colored cloth immediately before the pure drink supports purity saturating recipients as dye saturates fabric. B cases retain durable springs, group assignment, source-secured service, or coercive inward transfer, but stop below A when the access effect is retrospective or partly supplied by neighboring clauses.

**ورد** focuses one stage earlier on **entry into a water point and its ordered encounter**. The two-member cohort is unusually exact, and all four retained branches are A. At 12:19, the caravan dispatches a vanguard whose reaching the well is recursively completed when his lowered bucket reaches the boy. At 28:23, Moses' geographic arrival becomes entry into an explicit watering schedule of delayed turns and ordered succession. The Midian scene demonstrates the lexical division cleanly: **ورد** frames entry into the schedule, **ماء** names the socially contested resource, and **سقي** frames the act that removes the access barrier.

**غرق** marks the **overwhelming result and the partition it completes**, not merely contact with a water body. All 13 proposals that causative drowning secondarily means killing are rejected because death is already its ordinary entailment. B-grade exhaustive readings require direct totalizers, a complementary remainder, or no-survivor wording; broad judgment or vengeance is insufficient. The exceptional A at 79:1 proves the value of cohort control: with no overt water event, governing drawing language plus the limit-marking intensive realizes the mechanical image of a bow drawn fully, rather than another judgment-drowning reading.

## What the controls show

The negative results are interpretively consequential. Ordinary rain-to-growth does not activate freshness without a maturation cue; ordinary sea travel does not activate a travel branch; literal breadth does not become expressive capacity without a transfer such as ink; drowning does not become secondary killing; punitive heat does not satisfy specialized bodily heat effects; and nearby color, cognition, travel, or totality language does not count unless it changes the target word. High-burden source-separated and homonymous routes usually remain C or Reject, with only tightly grammatical exceptions reaching B or A.

Six families had no frozen retained branch mentions: **wave**, **fresh_sweet**, **stale_water**, **well**, **split_emergence**, and **pouring**. No readings were added to fill them. Their primary profiles function as controls against upgrading vivid but ordinary enclosure, potability, preservation, abandoned access, source-emergence, or profuse descent.

## Method, coverage, and limits

Neutral cohort profiles were built from the assigned root packets, positioned QAC morphemes, and a centered five-ayah window (the focus ayah plus two ayat on either side, clipped at surah boundaries). Adjudication then evaluated every frozen retained branch mention without adding candidates. Support required a local trigger, economical attachment to the target, and a contribution beyond the cohort baseline. **A** marks the strongest locally anchored changes, **B** supported but less exact or less surprising changes, **C** exploratory attachments, and **Reject** failures of secondary activation.

Across 20 families, the corpus contains **259 indexed target morphemes in 238 family-level focus-ayah assignments**. **191 targets had at least one frozen candidate and 68 had explicit none**. All **263/263 frozen branch mentions** were adjudicated.

The **119 A/B decisions** are branch judgments, not independent passages: a focus ayah can contain multiple targets, a target can receive multiple branch grades, and an ayah can occur in more than one family. The frozen candidate set is not proof that every possible reading was proposed, and explicit none is not proof that resonance is impossible. Root-level QAC occurrence is morphological evidence rather than branch assignment. The local window favors close mechanisms and can miss longer-range structure, while small and singleton cohorts cannot establish robust recurrence. Finally, A and B preserve the ordinary contextual meaning; they identify an additional locally supported contribution rather than replace the primary reading.
