# batch_001 Water Secondary Resonance

## 2:22 — ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلْأَرْضَ فِرَٰشًا وَٱلسَّمَآءَ بِنَآءً وَأَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِۦ مِنَ ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ فَلَا تَجْعَلُوا۟ لِلَّهِ أَندَادًا وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Ordinary rainwater descends from the sky and is the means by which fruits are brought out as provision.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window sets this ordered, beneficial descent between the disorienting storm of 2:20 and the fire of 2:24; the repeated descent verb in نَزَّلْنَا for revelation (2:23) also makes material provision and the revealed challenge parallel gifts calling for recognition rather than rivals.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B002 presents the water as water made to appear abundantly from the sky; root_001458/B003 presents it as deliberately supplied water whose destination is human provision.

**Activation trace.** For root_001458/B002, أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ is the visible pouring-from-sky construction and فَأَخْرَجَ ... ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ shows its abundance becoming manifest in produce; the bridge is from descent to productive appearance, so مَآءً contributes a staged emergence of provision. For root_001458/B003, رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ supplies the beneficiary and فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِ supplies the directed result; the bridge is purposeful delivery, so the water reads not only as rain but as provision actively conveyed through rain.

**Negative evidence.** The mention of fruits does not by itself activate root_001458/B006 because no ripening, sheen, or sweetness cue appears. Nothing locally supports reproductive fluid, plating, crystal, or heart-dullness branches.

## 2:74 — ثُمَّ قَسَتْ قُلُوبُكُم مِّنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ فَهِىَ كَٱلْحِجَارَةِ أَوْ أَشَدُّ قَسْوَةً وَإِنَّ مِنَ ٱلْحِجَارَةِ لَمَا يَتَفَجَّرُ مِنْهُ ٱلْأَنْهَٰرُ وَإِنَّ مِنْهَا لَمَا يَشَّقَّقُ فَيَخْرُجُ مِنْهُ ٱلْمَآءُ وَإِنَّ مِنْهَا لَمَا يَهْبِطُ مِنْ خَشْيَةِ ٱللَّهِ وَمَا ٱللَّهُ بِغَٰفِلٍ عَمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ

### مَآءُ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Literal water comes out of some stones when they split, one of three stone responses used to expose the greater hardness of the addressed hearts.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding revival of the dead and the following concealment and distortion of understood speech turn the stone comparison into a test of responsiveness: inert matter can open, release, and descend, while informed human hearts resist.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B002 is activated as water appearing from within split ground or stone. In a contrastive, not literal, role, root_001458/B008 is also activated as the idiom of heart-water associated with dullness.

**Activation trace.** For root_001458/B002, يَشَّقَّقُ فَيَخْرُجُ مِنْهُ ٱلْمَآءُ explicitly sequences splitting and water's appearance, reinforced by rivers bursting out; the water therefore marks hidden responsiveness becoming visible. For root_001458/B008, قُلُوبُكُم names the heart, while تَعْقِلُونَ in 2:73 and 2:76 and عَقَلُوهُ in 2:75 frame a failure of understanding; juxtaposing those cues with water-bearing stone supplies the abductive bridge, so the target water becomes a counter-image to dull “heart-water”: the stone's water issues in response, whereas the hard heart's cognition does not.

**Negative evidence.** يُحَرِّفُونَهُ in 2:75 concerns distortion, but without a coating, metallic sheen, or display construction it does not meet the higher burden for root_001458/B005. No watering act, reproductive setting, ripening image, or crystal cue appears.

## 2:164 — إِنَّ فِى خَلْقِ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَٱخْتِلَٰفِ ٱلَّيْلِ وَٱلنَّهَارِ وَٱلْفُلْكِ ٱلَّتِى تَجْرِى فِى ٱلْبَحْرِ بِمَا يَنفَعُ ٱلنَّاسَ وَمَآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مِن مَّآءٍ فَأَحْيَا بِهِ ٱلْأَرْضَ بَعْدَ مَوْتِهَا وَبَثَّ فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ دَآبَّةٍ وَتَصْرِيفِ ٱلرِّيَٰحِ وَٱلسَّحَابِ ٱلْمُسَخَّرِ بَيْنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ لَءَايَٰتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يَعْقِلُونَ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is the broad body of water through which ships travel carrying what benefits people.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Sea travel is one link in a tightly governed creation-wide sequence, while 2:165–166 contrasts that governance with misplaced attachments whose connecting means are finally cut.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The cosmic catalogue and مِن كُلِّ دَآبَّةٍ amplify the scale of the ayah, but they do not provide a sufficiently word-local trigger for root_000086/B002 beyond the sea's already primary breadth. تَقَطَّعَتْ in 2:166 is too remote from the sea word to activate the specialized wide-cleft branch root_000086/B003.

**Negative evidence.** There is no local salt, exposed meeting, water-holding hollow, red depth, bewilderment, sea-relation form, chance encounter, wasting disease, medical crisis, large belly, or unquenched-thirst cue.

### مَّآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater sent down from the sky revives dead earth and enables animal life to spread upon it.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The water is not an isolated marvel: it joins ship movement, alternating time, winds, clouds, life, and rational recognition in one governed system, against the rival loyalties exposed in the following ayat.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B002 activates water's appearing through sky-pouring; root_001458/B003 activates purposeful supply.

**Activation trace.** For root_001458/B002, أَنزَلَ ... مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ and the suspended cloud make sky-to-earth appearance explicit, while revival and the spread of every creature register its abundance; the target gains the force of water entering a dead environment and becoming visibly productive. For root_001458/B003, أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ and فَأَحْيَا بِهِ identify a supplier, instrument, and intended beneficiary-world; the bridge is directed life-supply, making the water an administered means within the larger order.

**Negative evidence.** Life and creatures alone do not supply the womb/deposition cues required for root_001458/B004. No fruit-ripening, plating, clarity, or heart-dullness trigger appears.

## 4:43 — يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تَقْرَبُوا۟ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَأَنتُمْ سُكَٰرَىٰ حَتَّىٰ تَعْلَمُوا۟ مَا تَقُولُونَ وَلَا جُنُبًا إِلَّا عَابِرِى سَبِيلٍ حَتَّىٰ تَغْتَسِلُوا۟ وَإِن كُنتُم مَّرْضَىٰٓ أَوْ عَلَىٰ سَفَرٍ أَوْ جَآءَ أَحَدٌ مِّنكُم مِّنَ ٱلْغَآئِطِ أَوْ لَٰمَسْتُمُ ٱلنِّسَآءَ فَلَمْ تَجِدُوا۟ مَآءً فَتَيَمَّمُوا۟ صَعِيدًا طَيِّبًا فَٱمْسَحُوا۟ بِوُجُوهِكُمْ وَأَيْدِيكُمْ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ كَانَ عَفُوًّا غَفُورًا

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Ordinary water is the normally sought material for ritual washing; when it cannot be found, clean earth and wiping provide the stated alternative.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window surrounds bodily preparation with truthful testimony, non-concealment, awareness of one's speech, and warnings about misguiding the path. Purity before prayer is thus situated inside accountable cognition and speech, not reduced to material washing.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** تَغْتَسِلُوا۟ and the fallback مَسْح action clarify the primary ritual function. They do not show the watering, pouring into, or provisioning construction specific enough to retain root_001458/B003; the target itself occurs precisely under non-availability, فَلَمْ تَجِدُوا۟.

**Negative evidence.** مَا تَقُولُونَ and حَدِيثًا do not describe sweet speech for root_001458/B006, and concealment or misguidance does not supply the coating/display image of root_001458/B005. No reproductive, abundance, crystal, or heart-water cue qualifies.

## 5:6 — يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ إِذَا قُمْتُمْ إِلَى ٱلصَّلَوٰةِ فَٱغْسِلُوا۟ وُجُوهَكُمْ وَأَيْدِيَكُمْ إِلَى ٱلْمَرَافِقِ وَٱمْسَحُوا۟ بِرُءُوسِكُمْ وَأَرْجُلَكُمْ إِلَى ٱلْكَعْبَيْنِ وَإِن كُنتُمْ جُنُبًا فَٱطَّهَّرُوا۟ وَإِن كُنتُم مَّرْضَىٰٓ أَوْ عَلَىٰ سَفَرٍ أَوْ جَآءَ أَحَدٌ مِّنكُم مِّنَ ٱلْغَآئِطِ أَوْ لَٰمَسْتُمُ ٱلنِّسَآءَ فَلَمْ تَجِدُوا۟ مَآءً فَتَيَمَّمُوا۟ صَعِيدًا طَيِّبًا فَٱمْسَحُوا۟ بِوُجُوهِكُمْ وَأَيْدِيكُم مِّنْهُ مَا يُرِيدُ ٱللَّهُ لِيَجْعَلَ عَلَيْكُم مِّنْ حَرَجٍ وَلَٰكِن يُرِيدُ لِيُطَهِّرَكُمْ وَلِيُتِمَّ نِعْمَتَهُۥ عَلَيْكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Ordinary water is the normal material for washing the named body parts and attaining bodily ritual purity; clean earth substitutes when water is unavailable.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The surrounding ayat place this bodily regimen among wholesome food, disciplined sexual relations, covenant fidelity, gratitude, and impartial justice. The material practice belongs to a wider pattern of clean, bounded, obedient conduct.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B003 is retained as the branch of water brought to and applied to a person.

**Activation trace.** The commands فَٱغْسِلُوا۟ وُجُوهَكُمْ وَأَيْدِيَكُمْ and فَٱطَّهَّرُوا۟ make directed application to the body visible, and لِيُطَهِّرَكُمْ states its role. The abductive bridge is from water as a found substance to water conveyed onto named recipients for an intended result; the target therefore contributes enacted purification, not mere availability.

**Negative evidence.** Faces alone do not establish the sheen/freshness branch root_001458/B006. No abundance, reproductive deposition, plating, crystal, or heart-dullness trigger passes the standard.

## 6:99 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَخْرَجْنَا بِهِۦ نَبَاتَ كُلِّ شَىْءٍ فَأَخْرَجْنَا مِنْهُ خَضِرًا نُّخْرِجُ مِنْهُ حَبًّا مُّتَرَاكِبًا وَمِنَ ٱلنَّخْلِ مِن طَلْعِهَا قِنْوَانٌ دَانِيَةٌ وَجَنَّٰتٍ مِّنْ أَعْنَابٍ وَٱلزَّيْتُونَ وَٱلرُّمَّانَ مُشْتَبِهًا وَغَيْرَ مُتَشَٰبِهٍ ٱنظُرُوٓا۟ إِلَىٰ ثَمَرِهِۦٓ إِذَآ أَثْمَرَ وَيَنْعِهِۦٓ إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكُمْ لَءَايَٰتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يُؤْمِنُونَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater sent from the sky is the means by which diverse green growth, grain, dates, grapes, olives, pomegranates, and fruit develop.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Human origination in 6:98 and the rejection in 6:100–101 of invented divine sons, daughters, consort, and child frame vegetal generation as evidence of God's creative agency without projecting creaturely reproduction onto God.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B002 activates abundant water appearing from the sky and within developing produce; root_001458/B006 activates watery freshness and ripening in fruit. In a contrastive role, root_001458/B004 is activated by the surrounding procreative vocabulary.

**Activation trace.** For root_001458/B002, أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ initiates the water's appearance and the repeated فَأَخْرَجْنَا / نُّخْرِجُ sequence tracks it becoming abundant life through multiple produce forms; water gains a cascading, internally productive presence. For root_001458/B006, خَضِرًا, ثَمَرِهِۦٓ إِذَآ أَثْمَرَ, and especially يَنْعِهِۦٓ visibly name freshness and ripening; the target water consequently contributes the moisture-filled sheen and maturity the observer is told to inspect. For root_001458/B004, أَنشَأَكُم مِّن نَّفْسٍ وَٰحِدَةٍ (6:98), بَنِينَ وَبَنَٰتٍ (6:100), and وَلَدٌ / صَٰحِبَةٌ (6:101) supply an unusually dense generation frame; the bridge is contrast, so rainwater's life-generation highlights divine creation while the branch's male-fluid mechanism remains creaturely and is expressly inappropriate as an account of God.

**Negative evidence.** The retained root_001458/B004 is not substituted for rain and does not claim that مَآءً denotes reproductive fluid here. No local coating, crystal, or heart-dullness cue qualifies.

## 7:50 — وَنَادَىٰٓ أَصْحَٰبُ ٱلنَّارِ أَصْحَٰبَ ٱلْجَنَّةِ أَنْ أَفِيضُوا۟ عَلَيْنَا مِنَ ٱلْمَآءِ أَوْ مِمَّا رَزَقَكُمُ ٱللَّهُ قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ حَرَّمَهُمَا عَلَى ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ

### مَآءِ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The people of the Fire ask the people of the Garden for some literal water to relieve them, but it and the Garden's provision are denied them.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Recognition by marks, the announcement of mercy, and the later pairing of guidance with mercy make the requested water a concrete boundary marker of mercy; worldly social accumulation has become useless, while the smallest relief depends on a provision they rejected.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B003 is retained as water supplied by pouring to a person.

**Activation trace.** أَفِيضُوا۟ عَلَيْنَا is an explicit command to pour or cause flow upon the speakers, مِنَ ٱلْمَآءِ names the requested substance, and رَزَقَكُمُ names it as received provision. The bridge is direct transitive delivery, so the target's contribution sharpens from desired water to a begged transfer of provision across a boundary that cannot be crossed.

**Negative evidence.** غَرَّتْهُمُ ٱلْحَيَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنْيَا in 7:51 supplies deception generally, but no coating, metallic wash, or false-display link ties it locally enough to the water for root_001458/B005. No abundance, reproduction, fruit-freshness, crystal, or heart-water cue qualifies.

## 7:57 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى يُرْسِلُ ٱلرِّيَٰحَ بُشْرًۢا بَيْنَ يَدَىْ رَحْمَتِهِۦ حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَقَلَّتْ سَحَابًا ثِقَالًا سُقْنَٰهُ لِبَلَدٍ مَّيِّتٍ فَأَنزَلْنَا بِهِ ٱلْمَآءَ فَأَخْرَجْنَا بِهِۦ مِن كُلِّ ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ كَذَٰلِكَ نُخْرِجُ ٱلْمَوْتَىٰ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَذَكَّرُونَ

### مَآءَ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater is sent down from a heavy cloud over dead land and brings out every kind of fruit, furnishing the stated analogy for bringing out the dead.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Prayer, restraint from corruption, fear, hope, and nearness of mercy precede the meteorological sequence; the following good/bad land contrast shows that the same incoming mercy discloses the receiving ground's quality. Rain is therefore both revival image and diagnostic event.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B002 activates water's abundant sky-borne appearance; root_001458/B003 activates directed water-supply.

**Activation trace.** For root_001458/B002, سَحَابًا ثِقَالًا and فَأَنزَلْنَا بِهِ ٱلْمَآءَ visibly stage accumulated water appearing from a heavy cloud, with مِن كُلِّ ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ registering its abundant outcome; the target gains the dimension of stored abundance released. For root_001458/B003, سُقْنَٰهُ لِبَلَدٍ مَّيِّتٍ gives the cloud a destination and فَأَنزَلْنَا ... فَأَخْرَجْنَا supplies the delivery-result sequence; the bridge is purposeful conveyance to needy ground, so the water becomes administered mercy rather than undirected rainfall.

**Negative evidence.** The fruits are outputs, but no ripening, moisture-filled sheen, or sweetness cue establishes root_001458/B006. No reproductive, plating, crystal, or heart-dullness branch passes the standard.

## 8:11 — إِذْ يُغَشِّيكُمُ ٱلنُّعَاسَ أَمَنَةً مِّنْهُ وَيُنَزِّلُ عَلَيْكُم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً لِّيُطَهِّرَكُم بِهِۦ وَيُذْهِبَ عَنكُمْ رِجْزَ ٱلشَّيْطَٰنِ وَلِيَرْبِطَ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِكُمْ وَيُثَبِّتَ بِهِ ٱلْأَقْدَامَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Literal rainwater descends upon the believers to cleanse them, remove satanic contamination, bind their hearts, and steady their feet.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window integrates sleep, angelic reinforcement, inward reassurance, bodily cleansing, stable footing, and battle command. Water is one material component in a coordinated answer to distress that reaches body, emotion, ground, and action.

**Water-root resonance.** root_001458/B002 activates water appearing through a descent from the sky; root_001458/B003 activates water deliberately supplied to people for specified effects.

**Activation trace.** For root_001458/B002, يُنَزِّلُ عَلَيْكُم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ is the explicit sky-to-recipient appearance construction; the target gains the force of timely water becoming present where it was needed. For root_001458/B003, عَلَيْكُم, لِّيُطَهِّرَكُم بِهِۦ, and the coordinated purpose clauses specify recipients, instrument, and results; the bridge is directed supply, so the rain reads as provision applied to cleanse and stabilize, not merely weather.

**Negative evidence.** قُلُوبِكُمْ and drowsiness do not activate root_001458/B008: the water's stated action is to bind and strengthen hearts, the opposite of the branch's dullness. No reproductive, plating, freshness, or crystal cue qualifies.

## 10:24 — إِنَّمَا مَثَلُ ٱلْحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنْيَا كَمَآءٍ أَنزَلْنَٰهُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ فَٱخْتَلَطَ بِهِۦ نَبَاتُ ٱلْأَرْضِ مِمَّا يَأْكُلُ ٱلنَّاسُ وَٱلْأَنْعَٰمُ حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَخَذَتِ ٱلْأَرْضُ زُخْرُفَهَا وَٱزَّيَّنَتْ وَظَنَّ أَهْلُهَآ أَنَّهُمْ قَٰدِرُونَ عَلَيْهَآ أَتَىٰهَآ أَمْرُنَا لَيْلًا أَوْ نَهَارًا فَجَعَلْنَٰهَا حَصِيدًا كَأَن لَّمْ تَغْنَ بِٱلْأَمْسِ كَذَٰلِكَ نُفَصِّلُ ٱلْءَايَٰتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَتَفَكَّرُونَ

### مَآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater is the basis of a similitude: it mixes with earth's vegetation, producing a flourishing scene that is abruptly reduced to stubble, like the fleeting life of this world.
