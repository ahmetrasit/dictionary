# batch_002 Water Secondary Resonance

## 11:7 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ فِى سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍ وَكَانَ عَرْشُهُۥ عَلَى ٱلْمَآءِ لِيَبْلُوَكُمْ أَيُّكُمْ أَحْسَنُ عَمَلًا وَلَئِن قُلْتَ إِنَّكُم مَّبْعُوثُونَ مِنۢ بَعْدِ ٱلْمَوْتِ لَيَقُولَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوٓا۟ إِنْ هَٰذَآ إِلَّا سِحْرٌ مُّبِينٌ

### مَآءِ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The word contributes ordinary water as that over which the throne was before or during the stated creation, while the ayah moves from creation to the testing of human action and resurrection.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding ayat place concealed and declared things, every creature's provision, and every resting and deposit under divine knowledge; water under the throne therefore enters a window about what is held, known, tested, and finally disclosed.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The hiding/declaring contrast in 11:5 and the unbelievers' dismissal of resurrection in 11:7 sharpen the ayah-level contrast between divine knowledge and human concealment, but they do not visibly turn the water into coating, false display, clarity, abundance, or any other accepted م و ه branch.

**Negative evidence.** `root_001458/B005` would require an actual coating or a more direct deceptive presentation, and `root_001458/B006` is not established merely by أَحْسَنُ عَمَلًا. No other branch passes the evidence standard.

## 11:43 — قَالَ سَـَٔاوِىٓ إِلَىٰ جَبَلٍ يَعْصِمُنِى مِنَ ٱلْمَآءِ قَالَ لَا عَاصِمَ ٱلْيَوْمَ مِنْ أَمْرِ ٱللَّهِ إِلَّا مَن رَّحِمَ وَحَالَ بَيْنَهُمَا ٱلْمَوْجُ فَكَانَ مِنَ ٱلْمُغْرَقِينَ

### مَآءِ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The water is the flood from which the son expects the mountain to protect him.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The attempted movement toward a mountain is interrupted by a mountain-like wave: the chosen refuge and the approaching danger share an image, while the next ayah shows that the same water obeys commands addressed to earth and sky.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` (water appearing, entering, or becoming abundant) and `root_001458/B003` (supplying or putting water into something) are retained.

**Activation trace.** For `root_001458/B002`, مَوْجٍ كَٱلْجِبَالِ, the intervening wave, drowning, and then غِيضَ ٱلْمَآءُ visibly stage overwhelming abundance followed by recession; the branch makes مَآءِ an engulfing quantity rather than a neutral substance. For `root_001458/B003`, the immediately following command يَٰٓأَرْضُ ٱبْلَعِى مَآءَكِ supplies the transitive placement cue: the water feared as uncontrollable in 11:43 is, by abductive contrast, water that can be directed into the earth.

**Negative evidence.** The concealment/coating, reproductive-fluid, sheen, crystal, and heart-dullness branches have no local trigger. The retained branches depend on the visible flood-volume and commanded-entry sequence, not on flood vocabulary alone.

### مَوْجُ — م و ج

**Primary reading.** The wave rises between father and son and completes the son's separation from the ark.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The wave is both barrier and moving terrain: it answers the son's proposed mountain refuge with the preceding image مَوْجٍ كَٱلْجِبَالِ and makes physical separation coincide with the failed separation from the unbelievers.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_001455/B001` already covers the ordinary wave and its surging turbulence; the running ship, mountain-like waves, and interposition intensify that primary image but do not activate a distinct accepted branch.

**Negative evidence.** The target inventory contains no branch other than `root_001455/B001`, so no secondary branch ID can be cited or invented.

### مُغْرَقِينَ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The son becomes one of those submerged and drowned by the flood.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The passive result closes a sequence of self-protection claims: neither isolation, ascent, nor a mountain can preserve him once the wave comes between them.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The wave, floodwater, and the designation مُغْرَقِينَ fully establish `root_001080/B001`, but that is the primary drowning branch. The earth's swallowing of its water in 11:44 does not explicitly say that the land is saturated, so it does not meet the burden for `root_001080/B003`.

**Negative evidence.** No visible bow-drawing, field takeover, small draught, ornamental bridle, or independently marked killing construction activates `root_001080/B002/B004/B005/B006/B008`. No secondary branch passes.

## 11:44 — وَقِيلَ يَٰٓأَرْضُ ٱبْلَعِى مَآءَكِ وَيَٰسَمَآءُ أَقْلِعِى وَغِيضَ ٱلْمَآءُ وَقُضِىَ ٱلْأَمْرُ وَٱسْتَوَتْ عَلَى ٱلْجُودِىِّ وَقِيلَ بُعْدًا لِّلْقَوْمِ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ

### مَآءَ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** In مَآءَكِ, the water is the earth's floodwater, commanded to be swallowed back into it.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The previous ayah presents water as what no mountain can repel; here earth and sky receive terse commands and the apparently unbounded flood is partitioned, withdrawn, and settled.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` (water entering or becoming abundant) and `root_001458/B003` (putting water into something) are retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_001458/B002` is locally triggered by the sequence of mountain-like wave, drowning, ٱبْلَعِى مَآءَكِ, and غِيضَ ٱلْمَآءُ: abundance visibly reverses into entry and depletion, making مَآءَكِ a controllable flood-volume. `root_001458/B003` is triggered by the transitive imperative ٱبْلَعِى with water as its object; the bridge is that commanded swallowing functions as directed placement into the earth, so the target contributes not only substance but routed content.

**Negative evidence.** No coating, reproductive, sheen, crystal, or heart-water cue appears. The two retained branches are supported by distinct local roles, intransitive entry/quantity and transitive directed placement.

### مَآءُ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** In وَغِيضَ ٱلْمَآءُ, the remaining floodwater is made to recede or diminish.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Its recession stands between paired commands to earth and sky and the completion of the affair, so hydraulic decline and judicial closure occur in one compressed sequence.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` is retained.

**Activation trace.** The visible contrast between مَوْجٍ كَٱلْجِبَالِ and غِيضَ ٱلْمَآءُ activates `root_001458/B002` as the abundance/entry branch. The abductive bridge is reversal: the same root-image that marks water becoming abundant also makes its ordered diminution salient, changing ٱلْمَآءُ from a static mass into a measured state that can be increased, absorbed, and reduced.

**Negative evidence.** Although the preceding imperative directs the earth to swallow water, this second target is grammatically the subject of recession rather than the object of supplying or pouring, so `root_001458/B003` is not separately retained here. The remaining branches lack triggers.

## 13:4 — وَفِى ٱلْأَرْضِ قِطَعٌ مُّتَجَٰوِرَٰتٌ وَجَنَّٰتٌ مِّنْ أَعْنَٰبٍ وَزَرْعٌ وَنَخِيلٌ صِنْوَانٌ وَغَيْرُ صِنْوَانٍ يُسْقَىٰ بِمَآءٍ وَٰحِدٍ وَنُفَضِّلُ بَعْضَهَا عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ فِى ٱلْأُكُلِ إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَءَايَٰتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يَعْقِلُونَ

### يُسْقَىٰ — س ق ي

**Primary reading.** The neighboring plots, vines, crops, and palms are irrigated with one water despite their differing produce.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Uniform input yields nonuniform food; the next ayah's incredulity about new creation is thereby framed by a visible creation in which one supply already produces sharply differentiated outcomes.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The crop list and بِمَآءٍ وَٰحِدٍ strongly specify irrigation, which belongs to the target's primary watering contribution and `root_000722/B003`; the nearby rivers support the setting but do not visibly establish a distinct source-allotment construction under `root_000722/B002`.

**Negative evidence.** There is no belly-fluid, prayer-for-rain, cloud, papyrus, dye-soaking, or forced-heart cue. No other س ق ي branch passes.

### مَآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The noun denotes the single ordinary water used to irrigate all the adjacent growth.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Its oneness is not flattening: the water becomes the stable common term against which differences in plant form and taste are made legible.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B003` (supplying water) and `root_001458/B006` (watery freshness or ripening in fruit) are retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_001458/B003` is directly triggered by يُسْقَىٰ بِمَآءٍ وَٰحِدٍ, which assigns the water an instrumental supplying role; this changes it from mere matter into a shared input distributed across plots. `root_001458/B006` is triggered by أَعْنَٰبٍ, نَخِيلٌ, الثَّمَرَات in 13:3, and preference فِى ٱلْأُكُلِ; because the branch explicitly includes grapes and date-fruit filling with ripening moisture, it makes the one water participate in the distinct freshness and maturation of each fruit.

**Negative evidence.** No local cue supports coating, reproductive fluid, crystal, or heart-dullness. Mere mention of rivers does not by itself establish the abundance/entry branch `root_001458/B002`.

## 13:14 — لَهُۥ دَعْوَةُ ٱلْحَقِّ وَٱلَّذِينَ يَدْعُونَ مِن دُونِهِۦ لَا يَسْتَجِيبُونَ لَهُم بِشَىْءٍ إِلَّا كَبَٰسِطِ كَفَّيْهِ إِلَى ٱلْمَآءِ لِيَبْلُغَ فَاهُ وَمَا هُوَ بِبَٰلِغِهِۦ وَمَا دُعَآءُ ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ إِلَّا فِى ضَلَٰلٍ

### مَآءِ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Water is the desired drink toward which a person stretches open hands, though it never reaches the mouth.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding thunder, angels, and thunderbolts respond within divine power, while the simile centers on a tiny failed transaction: hands, water, and mouth do not complete the requested movement. The following universal prostration deepens the contrast between responsive creation and unanswered invocation.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** بَاسِطِ كَفَّيْهِ, لِيَبْلُغَ فَاهُ, and the repeated negation of reaching make failed access to ordinary water precise. They do not show actual pouring, supplying, abundance, sheen, clarity, coating, reproductive fluid, or heart-water.

**Negative evidence.** `root_001458/B003` is specifically blocked by وَمَا هُوَ بِبَٰلِغِهِ: the desired transfer does not occur. The blindness/light and powerless-patrons contrasts in 13:16 are ayah-level interpretation, not triggers for `root_001458/B007` or `B008`.

## 13:17 — أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَسَالَتْ أَوْدِيَةٌۢ بِقَدَرِهَا فَٱحْتَمَلَ ٱلسَّيْلُ زَبَدًا رَّابِيًا وَمِمَّا يُوقِدُونَ عَلَيْهِ فِى ٱلنَّارِ ٱبْتِغَآءَ حِلْيَةٍ أَوْ مَتَٰعٍ زَبَدٌ مِّثْلُهُۥ كَذَٰلِكَ يَضْرِبُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلْحَقَّ وَٱلْبَٰطِلَ فَأَمَّا ٱلزَّبَدُ فَيَذْهَبُ جُفَآءً وَأَمَّا مَا يَنفَعُ ٱلنَّاسَ فَيَمْكُثُ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ كَذَٰلِكَ يَضْرِبُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلْأَمْثَالَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater descends from the sky, fills valleys according to their capacities, and bears rising foam.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Water-flow and metal-smelting produce the same disposable surface residue, allowing a physical sorting process to render the truth/falsehood contrast visible; 13:19 then names revelation itself as what has been sent down and recognized as truth.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` (water appearing or becoming abundant) and `root_001458/B005` (metal coating and deceptive display) are retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_001458/B002` is activated by أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ, the valleys flowing according to capacity, and the torrent carrying raised foam; the branch makes مَآءً a descending quantity whose locally varying abundance exposes capacity. `root_001458/B005` has an unusually visible double trigger: metal is heated لِحِلْيَةٍ أَوْ مَتَٰعٍ, its foam resembles the water's foam, and the sequence explicitly names ٱلْحَقَّ وَٱلْبَٰطِلَ. The bridge is surface versus substance, so water becomes an agent that carries a bright or swollen false surface away while benefit remains.

**Negative evidence.** The coating branch is retained because metalwork and explicit falsehood occur together, not merely because foam is visually suggestive. No reproductive-fluid, crystal, heart-dullness, or watering construction is visible.

### سَالَتْ — س ي ل

**Primary reading.** The valleys flow with the descended water, each according to its capacity.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The grammatical subject is the valleys rather than the water, making landscape capacity itself appear as flow; the later contrast between passing foam and what remains in earth gives that movement an epistemic role.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_000770/B001` already includes causing or undergoing flow and the channel of flow. Valley extension, foam carriage, and remaining benefit all elaborate that primary branch, but no separate accepted extension, sword-tang, tree, beard-side, or flank use is locally instantiated.

**Negative evidence.** The metal-heating comparison does not identify a sword, knife, handle, or tang and therefore cannot activate the higher-burden `root_000770/B003`. No other branch passes.

### سَّيْلُ — س ي ل

**Primary reading.** The torrent carries a layer of rising foam generated by the valley flow.

**Ayah-level surprise.** What looks elevated is precisely what the torrent removes, while what benefits people remains low in the earth; the torrent thus performs the parable's separation rather than merely supplying its scenery.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** Carrying foam is ordinary torrent behavior within `root_000770/B001`. Although the flow spreads through several valleys, the text does not supply the distinct blaze, troops, or analogous non-water extension required to make `root_000770/B002` secondary rather than an embellishment of the primary flow.

**Negative evidence.** The nearby metalwork lacks a tang construction, and no sayal tree or human side-part appears. No other branch passes.

## 14:16 — مِّن وَرَآئِهِۦ جَهَنَّمُ وَيُسْقَىٰ مِن مَّآءٍ صَدِيدٍ

### يُسْقَىٰ — س ق ي

**Primary reading.** The obstinate tyrant is made to drink water identified as صَدِيدٍ in Hell.

**Ayah-level surprise.** A verb normally associated with sustaining drink is inverted into punishment; the next ayah slows the act into repeated gulping that cannot be comfortably swallowed, with death approaching from every place but never completed.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000722/B010` is retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_000722/B010` concerns forcing disliked hostility into the heart as though making it drink. Here جَبَّارٍ عَنِيدٍ, the passive يُسْقَىٰ, صَدِيدٍ, and يَتَجَرَّعُهُۥ وَلَا يَكَادُ يُسِيغُهُۥ visibly supply hostility, coercion, aversion, and inward passage; the abductive reversal makes the metaphor bodily, changing the target from neutral administration into punishment forced inside.

**Negative evidence.** The branch is not retained from unpleasant taste alone; passive compulsion, enmity, and failed swallowing jointly anchor it. There is no source-allotment, irrigation, belly-fluid, rain-prayer, cloud, reed, or dye cue.

### مَّآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The word remains ordinary water at noun level, radically qualified by صَدِيدٍ as the punishing liquid being gulped.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Water, normally compatible with life, is placed between Hell and a death that repeatedly comes but does not occur; the life-bearing category becomes a vehicle for suspended dying.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The coercive drinking sequence changes the ayah's use of water, but it activates a secondary س ق ي branch rather than another م و ه branch. No visible entry/abundance, pouring, reproductive, coating, sheen, crystal, or heart-water construction occurs.

**Negative evidence.** صَدِيدٍ makes the liquid horrific but is not itself an accepted secondary branch of م و ه. No secondary water-root branch passes.

## 14:32 — ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ وَأَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِۦ مِنَ ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ وَسَخَّرَ لَكُمُ ٱلْفُلْكَ لِتَجْرِىَ فِى ٱلْبَحْرِ بِأَمْرِهِۦ وَسَخَّرَ لَكُمُ ٱلْأَنْهَٰرَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater is sent down and used to bring out fruits as provision.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window links believers' expenditure from provision to a cosmology of provision: descending water, extracted fruit, navigable sea, rivers, celestial cycles, and finally gifts too numerous to count.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` (water appearing or becoming abundant), `root_001458/B003` (supplying water), and `root_001458/B006` (watery ripening freshness) are retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_001458/B002` is triggered by أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ and the coordinated sea and rivers, making مَآءً part of system-scale appearing and abundance. `root_001458/B003` is triggered by the causal فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِۦ ... رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ: the water is deliberately supplied as the means of provision. `root_001458/B006` is triggered by ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ and رِزْقًا, with the branch's fruit-ripening moisture supplying the bridge; the target thus contributes freshness and maturation inside the fruit, not only rain outside it.

**Negative evidence.** The three retained branches have separate visible roles: descent/abundance, purposeful supply, and fruit maturation. No reproductive-fluid, coating, crystal, or heart-dullness cue appears.

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is the broad water body through which the subjected ships travel.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Sea, rivers, sun, moon, night, and day are all placed in a repeated سَخَّرَ لَكُم pattern, so the potentially trackless expanse becomes an ordered conduit within an inventory of provision.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B002` (expanse and breadth) and `root_000086/B009` (sea travel and relation) are retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_000086/B002` is activated by رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ, the sequence of sea, rivers, celestial service, and the uncountable نعمة in 14:34; because the branch explicitly extends breadth to wealth and pasture, the sea's expanse becomes a measure of provision's amplitude. `root_000086/B009` is directly triggered by ٱلْفُلْكَ لِتَجْرِىَ فِى ٱلْبَحْرِ; it assigns the sea the role of traveled medium, changing the target from water-body alone into an enabled route.

**Negative evidence.** No salt, cleft-ear, exposed meeting, water-holding hollow, womb-depth/redness, bewilderment, accidental encounter, disease, technical medical term, large belly, or unquenched thirst is visible. Breadth is retained through the explicit abundance frame, not from size alone.

## 15:22 — وَأَرْسَلْنَا ٱلرِّيَٰحَ لَوَٰقِحَ فَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَسْقَيْنَٰكُمُوهُ وَمَآ أَنتُمْ لَهُۥ بِخَٰزِنِينَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater is sent down from the sky and given to the addressees to drink, while their inability to store it is stressed.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding خزائن held with God, descent by known measure, human non-storage, and the following life/death claim make drinking water an entrusted release rather than a possession.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` (water appearing or descending abundantly), `root_001458/B003` (supplying water), and `root_001458/B004` (male fluid placed in the womb) are retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_001458/B002` is activated by فَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ and the خزائن/بِقَدَرٍ sequence: water appears as a measured release from stores. `root_001458/B003` is directly activated by فَأَسْقَيْنَٰكُمُوهُ, which makes the descended water a supplied drink rather than merely precipitation. `root_001458/B004` bears a higher burden, met here by the immediately adjacent ٱلرِّيَٰحَ لَوَٰقِحَ and the following نُحْىِۦ وَنُمِيتُ: the fertilizing construction supplies a reproductive bridge, so rainwater also resonates as generative fluid deposited into a receptive world.

**Negative evidence.** The reproductive branch is retained only because لَوَٰقِحَ, descent, and the life/death statement converge locally; rain alone would not suffice. No coating, sheen, crystal, or heart-dullness cue appears.

### أَسْقَيْ — س ق ي

**Primary reading.** In فَأَسْقَيْنَٰكُمُوهُ, God provides the descended water to the addressees as their drink or drinking supply.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The long pronominal construction delivers the sky-water all the way to its human recipients, then immediately denies that they control its خزائن; reception is grammatically close, ownership is denied.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The form أَسْقَيْنَا, its human object, the water object pronoun, and the storage contrast precisely realize the primary source-provision branch `root_000722/B002`. The window contains no separate irrigation, bodily fluid, prayer, rain-cloud noun, reed, dye, or forced-hostility construction.

**Negative evidence.** `root_000722/B001` is conceptually adjacent but adds no surprising role beyond the primary act of giving drink, and `root_000722/B010` has no aversion or hostility cue. No secondary branch passes.

## 16:10 — هُوَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً لَّكُم مِّنْهُ شَرَابٌ وَمِنْهُ شَجَرٌ فِيهِ تُسِيمُونَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater descends for the addressees, furnishing both drink and the vegetation in which animals graze.

**Ayah-level surprise.** One descent branches into human drink, grazing, crops, olives, palms, grapes, and all fruits, then joins the ordered night and celestial bodies; the window presents water as a branching infrastructure of guidance and sustenance.

### شَرَابٌ — ش ر ب

**Primary reading.** شَرَابٌ names the portion of descended water available as ordinary drink.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The repeated partition مِنْهُ شَرَابٌ وَمِنْهُ شَجَرٌ makes drink one visible phase of the same water that becomes grazing and diverse food, rather than an isolated cupful.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000783/B002` (allotted share of water), `root_000783/B007` (permeating or being imbued), and `root_000783/B014` (watered land or green growth) are retained.

**Activation trace.** `root_000783/B002` is activated by لَّكُم مِّنْهُ and the repeated مِنْهُ partition: شَرَابٌ is construed as the human share within one descended supply. `root_000783/B007` is activated by يُنۢبِتُ لَكُم بِهِ followed by crops, olives, palms, grapes, and fruits; the abductive bridge is plant uptake, so drinkable water also resonates as liquid permeating growth. `root_000783/B014` is triggered by شَجَرٌ فِيهِ تُسِيمُونَ and the next ayah's green-growth list, changing the target's contribution from consumed liquid to the watered land and vegetation that sustain grazing and food.

**Negative evidence.** No co-drinker, vessel/upper-room, unpleasant-water quality, body-part, neck-rope, craning, false charge, explicit understanding idiom, wealth-spending idiom, or waterskin treatment is visible. Plant permeation and watered growth are retained because the causal بِهِ and concrete vegetation list make both roles local.
