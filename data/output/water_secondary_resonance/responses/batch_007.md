# batch_007 Water Secondary Resonance

## 2:50 — وَإِذْ فَرَقْنَا بِكُمُ ٱلْبَحْرَ فَأَنجَيْنَٰكُمْ وَأَغْرَقْنَآ ءَالَ فِرْعَوْنَ وَأَنتُمْ تَنظُرُونَ

### بَحْرَ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The noun denotes the sea as the body of water that is divided so that Israel is delivered through it.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The surrounding sequence places this deliverance between Pharaoh's slaughter of the sons (2:49) and Israel's calf-taking followed by pardon (2:51-52). Passage through the sea therefore belongs to a sequence in which rescue does not eliminate the rescued community's moral exposure.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B003` (wide cleft) is retained. It supplements the sea's ordinary water-body sense with the image of an opened cleft.

**Activation trace.** The exact verbal trigger is `فَرَقْنَا ... ٱلْبَحْرَ`: the sea is visibly separated. The abductive bridge is that separation turns the broad water body into an opened interval, matching B003's cleft image. The target consequently contributes not only the obstacle crossed but the very opening fashioned for rescue.

**Negative evidence.** The sea is vast and the people travel through it, but no separate cue activates B002's transferred breadth or B009's lexical sea-travel/relationship forms. Saltiness, exposure, ground, redness, disease, thirst, and the higher-burden single-source branches have no local trigger.

### أَغْرَقْ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The form is causative: Pharaoh's people are made to drown in the sea while the delivered community looks on.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The drowning immediately reverses the preceding regime's `يُذَبِّحُونَ أَبْنَآءَكُمْ`: those who imposed lethal violence are themselves subjected to a lethal water-event.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B002` (killing by drowning, then killing more generally) is retained. The primary act of drowning remains intact, while the branch makes its killing function in the sequence explicit.

**Activation trace.** The visible trigger is the juxtaposition of `يُذَبِّحُونَ أَبْنَآءَكُمْ` (2:49) with `وَأَغْرَقْنَآ ءَالَ فِرْعَوْنَ` (2:50). The bridge is a reversal between two modes of imposed killing. B002 therefore makes the target contribute a judicial counterstroke to the prior slaughter, not merely disappearance beneath water.

**Negative evidence.** Nothing in the window points to tear-filled eyes or saturated ground (B003), bow-drawing (B004), field-wide absorption (B005), a drink measure (B006), or an ornamented bridle (B008).

## 5:96 — أُحِلَّ لَكُمْ صَيْدُ ٱلْبَحْرِ وَطَعَامُهُۥ مَتَٰعًا لَّكُمْ وَلِلسَّيَّارَةِ وَحُرِّمَ عَلَيْكُمْ صَيْدُ ٱلْبَرِّ مَا دُمْتُمْ حُرُمًا وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ٱلَّذِىٓ إِلَيْهِ تُحْشَرُونَ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea identifies the domain whose catch and food are permitted, in contrast with land game while the addressees are in a consecrated state.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The neighboring rules intensify restraint around attainable game, penalties, sanctuary, and final gathering. Within that regulated setting, the sea is presented as a permitted provision rather than as an unbounded exception to accountability.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B009` (sea travel and relation) is retained. The sea remains the source of catch, but its produce is also marked as provision for movement.

**Activation trace.** The local trigger is `مَتَٰعًا لَّكُمْ وَلِلسَّيَّارَةِ`, especially the explicit travelers `ٱلسَّيَّارَةِ`. The bridge is that food originating in the sea is assigned to people in transit, activating B009's sea-travel role. The target thus contributes a mobile provisioning domain, not only a fishing location.

**Negative evidence.** The land/sea contrast does not by itself establish transferred breadth (B002), and no cleaving, salt, exposed encounter, water-holding ground, bewilderment, disease, heat, belly, or thirst cue appears.

## 6:59 — وَعِندَهُۥ مَفَاتِحُ ٱلْغَيْبِ لَا يَعْلَمُهَآ إِلَّا هُوَ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا فِى ٱلْبَرِّ وَٱلْبَحْرِ وَمَا تَسْقُطُ مِن وَرَقَةٍ إِلَّا يَعْلَمُهَا وَلَا حَبَّةٍ فِى ظُلُمَٰتِ ٱلْأَرْضِ وَلَا رَطْبٍ وَلَا يَابِسٍ إِلَّا فِى كِتَٰبٍ مُّبِينٍ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is one of the two comprehensive terrestrial domains, paired with land, whose contents are known by God.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window moves from disputed timing and judgment (6:57-58) to knowledge extending from unseen keys and immense domains down to a falling leaf and a hidden grain, then to every deed and appointed death (6:60-61). Scale changes without creating any gap in knowledge.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B002` (expanse and breadth) is retained. The sea's ordinary geographic sense remains, while it also bears the role of a vast scope fully contained by divine knowledge.

**Activation trace.** The visible construction is the widening series `مَفَاتِحُ ٱلْغَيْبِ` / `مَا فِى ٱلْبَرِّ وَٱلْبَحْرِ` / leaf / grain / `رَطْبٍ` and `يَابِسٍ`, capped by `إِلَّا فِى كِتَٰبٍ مُّبِينٍ`. The bridge is that the sea occupies the broad-domain position in an exhaustive scale. B002 therefore makes the target contribute the idea that even maximal expanse is bounded within exact knowledge.

**Negative evidence.** Pairing sea with land is not a cleaving, travel, or open-exposure cue. The darkness belongs to the earth's grain and does not activate bewilderment in the sea word; the remaining specialized branches lack visible triggers.

## 6:63 — قُلْ مَن يُنَجِّيكُم مِّن ظُلُمَٰتِ ٱلْبَرِّ وَٱلْبَحْرِ تَدْعُونَهُۥ تَضَرُّعًا وَخُفْيَةً لَّئِنْ أَنجَىٰنَا مِنْ هَٰذِهِۦ لَنَكُونَنَّ مِنَ ٱلشَّٰكِرِينَ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is a domain of darkness and peril from which the endangered ask to be rescued, alongside the land.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The promised gratitude of secret, humble prayer is immediately followed by rescue from every distress and then renewed association of partners (6:64). The window exposes a sharp instability between crisis-recognition and post-rescue allegiance.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B008` (bewilderment and fear-struck stupefaction) is retained. It adds an experiential effect to the sea's ordinary peril without replacing the physical sea.

**Activation trace.** The triggers are `ظُلُمَٰتِ ... ٱلْبَحْرِ`, urgent `تَدْعُونَهُۥ تَضَرُّعًا وَخُفْيَةً`, and the generalizing `مِن كُلِّ كَرْبٍ` in 6:64. The bridge is that darkness and distress reduce the occupants to fearful, disoriented supplication, the state named by B008. The target consequently contributes a crisis-space that overwhelms ordinary self-possession and briefly clarifies dependence.

**Negative evidence.** Rescue at sea does not by itself activate B009 sea travel, since no ship, riding, or journey form appears. No local item supports cleaving, saltiness, exposure, ground, bodily wasting, medical heat, belly, or thirst.

## 6:97 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلنُّجُومَ لِتَهْتَدُوا۟ بِهَا فِى ظُلُمَٰتِ ٱلْبَرِّ وَٱلْبَحْرِ قَدْ فَصَّلْنَا ٱلْءَايَٰتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَعْلَمُونَ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is a dark navigational domain in which the stars serve as signs of direction, paired with land.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The focus on guidance is framed by successive acts of distinction: life from death, death from life, daybreak from night, and then detailed signs for knowing and understanding. Navigation becomes one instance of a larger pattern of making differences legible.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B003` (wide cleft) is retained. The sea remains water, while the window lets it resonate as a dark expanse opened into an intelligible route.

**Activation trace.** The especially visible cues are the repeated `فَالِقُ` in `فَالِقُ ٱلْحَبِّ وَٱلنَّوَىٰ` (6:95) and `فَالِقُ ٱلْإِصْبَاحِ` (6:96), followed by `فَصَّلْنَا ٱلْءَايَٰتِ` in the focus ayah. The bridge is the repeated image of splitting or distinguishing what was closed or dark. B003 therefore changes the target's contribution from a merely dark sea into a domain across which signs open distinguishable bearings.

**Negative evidence.** Stars used for guidance do not themselves establish B009's lexical sea-travel branch, because no vessel or sea-riding construction occurs. The following rain and vegetation affect the ayah's sign-pattern but do not activate the sea root's ground, salt, thirst, or other specialized branches.

## 7:138 — وَجَٰوَزْنَا بِبَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ ٱلْبَحْرَ فَأَتَوْا۟ عَلَىٰ قَوْمٍ يَعْكُفُونَ عَلَىٰٓ أَصْنَامٍ لَّهُمْ قَالُوا۟ يَٰمُوسَى ٱجْعَل لَّنَآ إِلَٰهًا كَمَا لَهُمْ ءَالِهَةٌ قَالَ إِنَّكُمْ قَوْمٌ تَجْهَلُونَ

### بَحْرَ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is the major boundary across which Israel is carried after the drowning of the opposing people.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Immediately after rescue, inheritance, and the destruction of Pharaoh's works, the travelers encounter idol-devotees and request a comparable god. Geographic passage is abruptly shown not to guarantee understanding.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B008` (bewilderment and stupefaction, including foolishness) is retained. The physical crossing remains primary, but the sea also marks a transition that exposes continuing cognitive disorientation.

**Activation trace.** The local trigger is Moses' direct diagnosis `إِنَّكُمْ قَوْمٌ تَجْهَلُونَ`, prompted immediately after `وَجَٰوَزْنَا ... ٱلْبَحْرَ` by the request for an idol. The bridge is that B008's foolish or bewildered state is overtly named as the response displayed on the far side of the sea. The target thus contributes an ironic threshold: they cross the water boundary while remaining mentally confounded.

**Negative evidence.** The prior drowning is an event of another root and does not by itself activate a sea-root branch. `جَٰوَزْنَا` indicates crossing but supplies no explicit cleft, and the wide inherited east-west land is ayah-level context rather than sufficient activation of B002 in this target.

## 7:163 — وَسْـَٔلْهُمْ عَنِ ٱلْقَرْيَةِ ٱلَّتِى كَانَتْ حَاضِرَةَ ٱلْبَحْرِ إِذْ يَعْدُونَ فِى ٱلسَّبْتِ إِذْ تَأْتِيهِمْ حِيتَانُهُمْ يَوْمَ سَبْتِهِمْ شُرَّعًا وَيَوْمَ لَا يَسْبِتُونَ لَا تَأْتِيهِمْ كَذَٰلِكَ نَبْلُوهُم بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَفْسُقُونَ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea locates the village and supplies the fish whose patterned coming becomes the occasion of the Sabbath test.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding command concerning a village is answered by verbal substitution and punishment (7:161-162); here the seaside village is tested through a temporal alternation in access to fish, followed by warning, rescue, and punishment (7:164-165). Place and provision become instruments that disclose response to command.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `حَاضِرَةَ ٱلْبَحْرِ` and the fish's coming on one class of days firmly connect the sea to location and provision, but neither construction supplies the distinctive form or image required for another `root_000086` branch.

**Negative evidence.** `حَاضِرَةَ` does not establish B005's higher-burden idiom of open, unscreened encounter, and `تَأْتِيهِمْ` is scheduled rather than B010's unintended human encounter. The alternation of days is a test pattern, not evidence for cleaving, breadth, travel, saltiness, bewilderment, or the specialized bodily branches.

## 10:22 — هُوَ ٱلَّذِى يُسَيِّرُكُمْ فِى ٱلْبَرِّ وَٱلْبَحْرِ حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا كُنتُمْ فِى ٱلْفُلْكِ وَجَرَيْنَ بِهِم بِرِيحٍ طَيِّبَةٍ وَفَرِحُوا۟ بِهَا جَآءَتْهَا رِيحٌ عَاصِفٌ وَجَآءَهُمُ ٱلْمَوْجُ مِن كُلِّ مَكَانٍ وَظَنُّوٓا۟ أَنَّهُمْ أُحِيطَ بِهِمْ دَعَوُا۟ ٱللَّهَ مُخْلِصِينَ لَهُ ٱلدِّينَ لَئِنْ أَنجَيْتَنَا مِنْ هَٰذِهِۦ لَنَكُونَنَّ مِنَ ٱلشَّٰكِرِينَ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is the domain through which God conveys people in ships, first under a favorable wind and then amid a surrounding storm.

**Ayah-level surprise.** A transition from tasted mercy and plotting (10:21) to pleasant sailing, terror, sincere prayer, rescue, and renewed transgression (10:22-23) makes changing conditions expose equally rapid changes in human posture.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B009` (sea travel and relation) and `root_000086/B008` (bewilderment and fear-struck stupefaction) are retained. Together they add the sea's roles as a traveled domain and as a setting that overturns composure.

**Activation trace.** For `root_000086/B009`, the triggers are `يُسَيِّرُكُمْ`, `فِى ٱلْفُلْكِ`, and `جَرَيْنَ بِهِم`; the explicit sequence of conveyance, vessel, and sailing activates the travel branch, so the target contributes an avenue of divinely enabled movement. For `root_000086/B008`, the triggers are the violent wind, wave `مِن كُلِّ مَكَانٍ`, the belief `أَنَّهُمْ أُحِيطَ بِهِمْ`, and crisis-prayer; the bridge is that total encirclement produces B008's fear-struck loss of ordinary confidence. The sea consequently also contributes the crisis-space in which easy joy becomes disoriented sincerity.

**Negative evidence.** `مِن كُلِّ مَكَانٍ` describes the wave's arrival but does not independently activate B002's transferred breadth. No exact cue supports the cleft, salt, exposed-place, ground, redness, disease, medical, belly, thirst, or accidental-encounter branches.

### مَوْجُ — م و ج

**Primary reading.** The wave is the turbulent sea movement that comes upon the ship from every direction and creates the sense of enclosure.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The wave reverses the passengers' joy in the favorable wind and becomes the immediate condition for exclusive prayer; after rescue, that exclusivity disappears into transgression.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The sole accepted inventory branch, `root_001455/B001`, is already the target's ordinary surging-water sense, visibly expressed by `ٱلْمَوْجُ مِن كُلِّ مَكَانٍ` after `رِيحٌ عَاصِفٌ`. It explains the primary turbulence and cannot serve as an additional branch.

**Negative evidence.** The inventory supplies no other `root_001455` branch. The surrounding human agitation and later transgression extend the ayah-level pattern, but they do not license an invented secondary branch.

## 10:90 — وَجَٰوَزْنَا بِبَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ ٱلْبَحْرَ فَأَتْبَعَهُمْ فِرْعَوْنُ وَجُنُودُهُۥ بَغْيًا وَعَدْوًا حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَدْرَكَهُ ٱلْغَرَقُ قَالَ ءَامَنتُ أَنَّهُۥ لَآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا ٱلَّذِىٓ ءَامَنَتْ بِهِۦ بَنُوٓا۟ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ وَأَنَا۠ مِنَ ٱلْمُسْلِمِينَ

### بَحْرَ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is crossed by Israel and then entered in pursuit by Pharaoh and his forces, where drowning overtakes him.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The accepted prayer for hardened hearts to see punishment (10:88-89) is answered in a scene where Pharaoh voices belief only once drowning reaches him; 10:91 then contrasts that present claim with prior rebellion, while 10:92 preserves his body as a sign.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B008` (bewilderment and fear-struck stupefaction) is retained. It adds the sea's power to produce a fear-driven collapse of Pharaoh's prior posture.

**Activation trace.** The triggers are the pursuit `بَغْيًا وَعَدْوًا`, the sudden limit `حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَدْرَكَهُ ٱلْغَرَقُ`, and the immediately ensuing profession `قَالَ ءَامَنتُ`. The bridge is that being overtaken within the sea converts defiant aggression into the fear-struck state covered by B008. The target therefore contributes the setting that stupefies the pursuer into belated acknowledgment, while remaining the physical sea.

**Negative evidence.** Crossing alone is insufficient for B003 cleaving because no split is stated in this window, and there is no vessel or sea-riding form for B009. The pursuit does not by itself establish B002's transferred breadth or its horse subcase; all other specialized branches lack a local trigger.

### غَرَقُ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The noun denotes the drowning that overtakes Pharaoh and brings his pursuit to its terminal point.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Drowning is positioned as the concrete arrival of the punishment requested in 10:88 and the answer confirmed in 10:89, yet the speech it elicits is rejected as belated in 10:91.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001080/B002` (killing by drowning) is retained. The event remains literal drowning, while the branch adds its role as an executed lethal judgment.

**Activation trace.** The visible sequence is `ٱلْعَذَابَ ٱلْأَلِيمَ` in Moses' prayer, `قَدْ أُجِيبَت دَّعْوَتُكُمَا`, and then `أَدْرَكَهُ ٱلْغَرَقُ`. The abductive bridge is that the drowning realizes the already requested and accepted punishment, fitting B002's killing-by-drowning role. The target consequently contributes not an accidental maritime death but the lethal completion of the answered judgment.

**Negative evidence.** `أَدْرَكَهُ` describes the drowning's arrival but is not enough to assign B005's separate field-absorption uses. No tears or saturated land, bow, drink measure, or ornamented bridle appears.

## 16:14 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى سَخَّرَ ٱلْبَحْرَ لِتَأْكُلُوا۟ مِنْهُ لَحْمًا طَرِيًّا وَتَسْتَخْرِجُوا۟ مِنْهُ حِلْيَةً تَلْبَسُونَهَا وَتَرَى ٱلْفُلْكَ مَوَاخِرَ فِيهِ وَلِتَبْتَغُوا۟ مِن فَضْلِهِۦ وَلَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ

### بَحْرَ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is a subjected water domain yielding fresh food and ornament and carrying ships through which people seek divine bounty.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window aligns the sea with subjected celestial cycles, varied earthly colors, stabilizing mountains, rivers, paths, and guiding marks (16:12-16). The sea's food, adornment, movement, commerce, and demanded gratitude form one part of a coordinated order rather than isolated resources.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B003` (wide cleft) and `root_000086/B009` (sea travel and relation) are retained. The sea remains the abundant water body; the branches add the opened track made by ships and its role as a traveled domain.

**Activation trace.** For `root_000086/B003`, the visible trigger is `ٱلْفُلْكَ مَوَاخِرَ فِيهِ`: the ships are presented as cutting their way through the sea. The bridge from that cutting image to B003's wide cleft makes the target contribute a medium visibly opened into tracks of passage. For `root_000086/B009`, `ٱلْفُلْكَ`, movement `فِيهِ`, and `لِتَبْتَغُوا۟ مِن فَضْلِهِۦ` jointly activate sea travel; this makes the target contribute a route for seeking bounty as well as a source from which goods are extracted.

**Negative evidence.** Fresh flesh does not activate saltiness, and extraction of ornament does not activate the unrelated decorated-bridle branch of another root. The nearby rivers and paths support the ayah-level order but do not establish the sea root's ground, breadth, exposure, bewilderment, disease, belly, or thirst branches.
