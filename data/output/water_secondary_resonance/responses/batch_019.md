# batch_019 Water Secondary Resonance

## 28:40 — فَأَخَذْنَٰهُ وَجُنُودَهُۥ فَنَبَذْنَٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ فَٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَٰقِبَةُ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ

### يَمِّ — ي م م

**Primary reading.** The **يَمّ** is the great body of water into which Pharaoh and his forces are seized and cast; it supplies the concrete place and instrument of their destruction.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window reverses Pharaoh's attempted ascent and self-exaltation: after ordering a tower so that he might look **إِلَىٰ** Moses's God and assuming no return **إِلَيْنَا**, he is forcibly sent downward into the sea. The following **يَدْعُونَ إِلَى ٱلنَّارِ** extends that reversal from a physical end to the destination toward which his leadership points.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001697/B001` (deliberately aiming toward a target) is retained. It makes the **يَمّ** not merely the water in which they happen to perish but the deliberately assigned endpoint of the counter-action against them.

**Activation trace.** Trigger: the directed construction **فَأَخَذْنَٰهُ ... فَنَبَذْنَٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ**, reinforced by the window's repeated orientations **أَطَّلِعُ إِلَىٰ**, **إِلَيْنَا لَا يُرْجَعُونَ**, and **يَدْعُونَ إِلَى**. Role: B001 contributes intentional targeting. Bridge: the sequence contrasts Pharaoh's chosen aim with the destination chosen for him. Concrete change: **يَمّ** reads as a judicially aimed terminus as well as great water.

**Negative evidence.** The nearby **ٱلطِّينِ** alone does not activate `root_001697/B002`: no clean earth, prayer, face, or hand-wiping construction appears. Nothing locally cues the bird branch `B004` or the proper-name/place branch `B005`.

## 51:40 — فَأَخَذْنَٰهُ وَجُنُودَهُۥ فَنَبَذْنَٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ وَهُوَ مُلِيمٌ

### يَمِّ — ي م م

**Primary reading.** The **يَمّ** is the great water into which Pharaoh and his army are cast while he is blameworthy.

**Ayah-level surprise.** A compact sending-and-rejection sequence frames the drowning: Moses is **أَرْسَلْنَٰهُ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ**, Pharaoh turns away, and Pharaoh with his forces is then seized and cast away. The next example replaces destructive water with a barren wind that leaves everything like decay, making different created media execute parallel judgments.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001697/B001` (deliberately aiming toward a target) is retained. It adds to the sea the force of an appointed destination: Pharaoh's rejection of the messenger's directed mission ends in his own directed casting.

**Activation trace.** Trigger: **أَرْسَلْنَٰهُ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ** followed by **فَأَخَذْنَٰهُ ... فَنَبَذْنَٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ**. Role: B001 marks deliberate orientation toward an endpoint. Bridge: the matched sequence of sending toward Pharaoh and casting Pharaoh into the sea makes destination structurally conspicuous. Concrete change: **يَمّ** becomes the selected terminus of judgment, not merely its physical setting.

**Negative evidence.** No clean earth or ritual act supports `root_001697/B002`; no bird image supports `B004`; no proper name or relevant place-name supports `B005`.

## 11:42 — وَهِىَ تَجْرِى بِهِمْ فِى مَوْجٍ كَٱلْجِبَالِ وَنَادَىٰ نُوحٌ ٱبْنَهُۥ وَكَانَ فِى مَعْزِلٍ يَٰبُنَىَّ ٱرْكَب مَّعَنَا وَلَا تَكُن مَّعَ ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ

### مَوْجٍ — م و ج

**Primary reading.** **مَوْجٍ** denotes the immense, turbulent waves through which the vessel carries its passengers, their scale rendered by **كَٱلْجِبَالِ**.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The mountain-like wave exposes the son's proposed mountain refuge as no refuge at all. It also moves from surrounding turbulence to a precise separating agent: **وَحَالَ بَيْنَهُمَا ٱلْمَوْجُ**, turning a vast environmental force into the barrier between father and son.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The visible cues **تَجْرِى**, **كَٱلْجِبَالِ**, **حَالَ بَيْنَهُمَا**, and **مِنَ ٱلْمُغْرَقِينَ** intensify the ordinary wave image already covered by `root_001455/B001`; they do not activate another branch.

**Negative evidence.** The inventory contains only `root_001455/B001`, which supplies the primary sense and its extensions to turbulence; there is no distinct accepted branch to retain secondarily.

## 18:99 — وَتَرَكْنَا بَعْضَهُمْ يَوْمَئِذٍ يَمُوجُ فِى بَعْضٍ وَنُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَجَمَعْنَٰهُمْ جَمْعًا

### يَمُوجُ — م و ج

**Primary reading.** **يَمُوجُ فِى بَعْضٍ** depicts people surging into and intermingling with one another like turbulent waves.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding barrier cannot be climbed or pierced, then is promised to be leveled; immediately the formerly bounded populations become an unbounded human surge. The motion is then arrested by **فَجَمَعْنَٰهُمْ جَمْعًا**, so uncontrolled mutual mixing gives way to a total, divinely effected gathering.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** **بَعْضَهُمْ ... فِى بَعْضٍ** visibly realizes the intermingling component of `root_001455/B001`, while the contrast with **فَجَمَعْنَٰهُمْ جَمْعًا** sharpens its disorder. Both remain within the target's primary accepted branch.

**Negative evidence.** The inventory supplies only `root_001455/B001`; no separate branch exists for a secondary assignment.

## 31:32 — وَإِذَا غَشِيَهُم مَّوْجٌ كَٱلظُّلَلِ دَعَوُا۟ ٱللَّهَ مُخْلِصِينَ لَهُ ٱلدِّينَ فَلَمَّا نَجَّىٰهُمْ إِلَى ٱلْبَرِّ فَمِنْهُم مُّقْتَصِدٌ وَمَا يَجْحَدُ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَآ إِلَّا كُلُّ خَتَّارٍ كَفُورٍ

### مَّوْجٌ — م و ج

**Primary reading.** **مَوْجٌ** is an overwhelming sea wave covering the voyagers like canopies and provoking sincere invocation.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The wave's physical instability uncovers instability in the people: under its cover they call with exclusive devotion, but after deliverance to land some retreat to a merely measured stance and others deny. The preceding calm sailing **بِنِعْمَتِ ٱللَّهِ** and the following warning against deception make the wave a moment of disclosure rather than only danger.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** **غَشِيَهُم**, **كَٱلظُّلَلِ**, and the sea-to-land sequence intensify the surging-water image of `root_001455/B001`; the oscillation in devotion is an ayah-level analogy, not evidence for another water-root branch.

**Negative evidence.** The inventory contains only `root_001455/B001`. No additional accepted branch is available for secondary resonance.

## 12:49 — ثُمَّ يَأْتِى مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ عَامٌ فِيهِ يُغَاثُ ٱلنَّاسُ وَفِيهِ يَعْصِرُونَ

### يُغَاثُ — غ ي ث

**Primary reading.** **يُغَاثُ ٱلنَّاسُ** announces that the people will be relieved after the seven severe years; the passive form makes received deliverance the clause's ordinary contribution.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Relief is not abstract or momentary. The window moves from sustained sowing, harvesting, and guarded grain through years that consume stored provision, then to a year in which people again press produce. The single year reverses the agency of the devouring years and restores productive human action.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001118/B001` (life-giving rain) and `root_001118/B002` (rain-grown vegetation) are retained. B001 locates the relief in renewed precipitation; B002 makes it relief embodied in restored crops and their yield.

**Activation trace.** For B001, trigger: the agricultural drought/recovery sequence **تَزْرَعُونَ ... سَبْعٌ شِدَادٌ ... عَامٌ فِيهِ يُغَاثُ**. Role: rain is the enabling medium. Bridge: after years that consume stored harvests, renewed agricultural activity requires restored water. Concrete change: **يُغَاثُ** becomes meteorologically concrete relief. For B002, trigger: **سُنۢبُلِهِ**, **حَصَدتُّمْ**, and especially the resumed **يَعْصِرُونَ**. Role: vegetation is the visible product of relief. Bridge: pressing presupposes renewed produce after scarcity. Concrete change: the verb includes recovery of fruitfulness, not only escape from distress.

**Negative evidence.** No cloud is named or imaged strongly enough for `root_001118/B003`. No horse or racing sequence supports the single-source `B005`.

## 31:34 — إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ عِندَهُۥ عِلْمُ ٱلسَّاعَةِ وَيُنَزِّلُ ٱلْغَيْثَ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا فِى ٱلْأَرْحَامِ وَمَا تَدْرِى نَفْسٌ مَّاذَا تَكْسِبُ غَدًا وَمَا تَدْرِى نَفْسٌۢ بِأَىِّ أَرْضٍ تَمُوتُ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ عَلِيمٌ خَبِيرٌۢ

### غَيْثَ — غ ي ث

**Primary reading.** **ٱلْغَيْثَ** is the rain that God sends down, placed among matters of divine knowledge and action inaccessible to ordinary human foresight.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Rain is framed between the unknown Hour and what is hidden in wombs, future earning, and the land of one's death. It therefore contributes not only water but a concrete instance of life-shaping futurity that humans cannot master.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001118/B004` (relief of one in distress) is retained. It lets the rain carry the remembered force of deliverance, not precipitation alone.

**Activation trace.** Trigger: two ayat earlier, those covered by a wave **دَعَوُا۟ ٱللَّهَ مُخْلِصِينَ** and **نَجَّىٰهُمْ إِلَى ٱلْبَرِّ**. Role: B004 supplies distress followed by divine rescue. Bridge: the same local window moves from desperate invocation and deliverance to God sending **غيث**, another event beyond human control. Concrete change: the rain reads as divinely administered succor within the sequence of unknowable outcomes.

**Negative evidence.** No vegetation cue supports `root_001118/B002`, and no cloud is visible for `B003`. **تَكْسِبُ غَدًا** is not horse-running and does not meet the high burden for the single-source `B005`.

## 42:28 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى يُنَزِّلُ ٱلْغَيْثَ مِنۢ بَعْدِ مَا قَنَطُوا۟ وَيَنشُرُ رَحْمَتَهُۥ وَهُوَ ٱلْوَلِىُّ ٱلْحَمِيدُ

### غَيْثَ — غ ي ث

**Primary reading.** **ٱلْغَيْثَ** is rain sent down after people have despaired, renewing the land and their prospects.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding ayah says provision is not simply spread without limit but sent down **بِقَدَرٍ مَّا يَشَآءُ**; the rain then arrives after despair and is paired with the spreading of mercy. Thus its delay and measure, not only its abundance, belong to its beneficent function.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001118/B004` (relief of one in distress) is retained. It changes **غيث** from merely awaited precipitation into the enacted release of people whose hope has failed.

**Activation trace.** Trigger: the explicit sequence **مِنۢ بَعْدِ مَا قَنَطُوا۟ وَيَنشُرُ رَحْمَتَهُۥ**, supported by **وَهُوَ ٱلْوَلِىُّ**. Role: B004 supplies rescue and release from distress. Bridge: despair names the distressed state, and the coordinated spreading of mercy names its reversal. Concrete change: the target rain becomes personal and providential relief as well as water.

**Negative evidence.** No plant or herbage appears for `root_001118/B002`; descent alone does not establish the cloud-naming branch `B003`; no horse or successive running supports the single-source `B005`.

## 57:20 — ٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّمَا ٱلْحَيَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنْيَا لَعِبٌ وَلَهْوٌ وَزِينَةٌ وَتَفَاخُرٌۢ بَيْنَكُمْ وَتَكَاثُرٌ فِى ٱلْأَمْوَٰلِ وَٱلْأَوْلَٰدِ كَمَثَلِ غَيْثٍ أَعْجَبَ ٱلْكُفَّارَ نَبَاتُهُۥ ثُمَّ يَهِيجُ فَتَرَىٰهُ مُصْفَرًّا ثُمَّ يَكُونُ حُطَٰمًا وَفِى ٱلْءَاخِرَةِ عَذَابٌ شَدِيدٌ وَمَغْفِرَةٌ مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَرِضْوَٰنٌ وَمَا ٱلْحَيَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنْيَآ إِلَّا مَتَٰعُ ٱلْغُرُورِ

### غَيْثٍ — غ ي ث

**Primary reading.** **غَيْثٍ** is rain in the simile of worldly life: it produces growth that delights its observers before yellowing and becoming debris.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The rain image does not end at life-giving growth; the repeated **ثُمَّ** forces attention through attraction, ripening or agitation, discoloration, and collapse. The next command **سَابِقُوٓا۟** redirects the competitive energy named by **تَفَاخُرٌ** and **تَكَاثُرٌ** away from transient increase toward forgiveness and the garden.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001118/B002` (rain-grown vegetation) and `root_001118/B005` (successive horse-running) are retained. B002 shifts **غيث** metonymically from falling rain toward the growth whose whole lifecycle the simile tracks. B005 adds an unusually rapid, successive-running cadence to that lifecycle and sharpens the following redirection of human racing.

**Activation trace.** For B002, trigger: the immediate possessive **نَبَاتُهُۥ**, followed by **يَهِيجُ**, **مُصْفَرًّا**, and **حُطَٰمًا**. Role: the rain-named vegetation becomes the image's visible subject. Bridge: the text explicitly moves from rain to its growth and then follows that growth. Concrete change: **غيث** bears the entire produced lifecycle, not just its initiating shower. For the higher-burden B005, trigger: the repeated succession **ثُمَّ ... ثُمَّ** followed immediately by **سَابِقُوٓا۟**. Role: B005 contributes one run following another. Bridge: sequential stages race to collapse, then the audience is told to race in another direction. Concrete change: **غيث** acquires a compressed forward momentum that exposes worldly flourishing as short-lived and redirects striving.

**Negative evidence.** No cloud appears for `root_001118/B003`. The mention of forgiveness does not itself show a distressed person's plea or rescue, so `B004` is not retained.

## 56:70 — لَوْ نَشَآءُ جَعَلْنَٰهُ أُجَاجًا فَلَوْلَا تَشْكُرُونَ

### أُجَاجًا — ء ج ج

**Primary reading.** **أُجَاجًا** describes the drinking water as intensely salty and bitter, hence a withheld drinkable blessing were God to make it so.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window places potable water and kindled fire in parallel questions of human versus divine production. The threatened transformation of drink into harshness prepares for the adjacent fire, while **فَلَوْلَا تَشْكُرُونَ** identifies ordinary drinkability itself as the overlooked gift.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000014/B002` (blazing, glowing, and intense heat) is retained. It adds a fire-like register of severity to the bitter salinity and binds the water warning to the immediately following fire scene.

**Activation trace.** Trigger: **أَفَرَءَيْتُمُ ٱلنَّارَ ٱلَّتِى تُورُونَ** directly after **أُجَاجًا**. Role: B002 supplies blazing and kindled heat. Bridge: the two adjacent **أَفَرَءَيْتُم** units juxtapose altered drinking water with fire humans kindle, and the root inventory itself recognizes heat as a distinct accepted branch. Concrete change: **أجاج** conveys not only undrinkable salt-bitterness but an almost burning harshness within a paired water/fire test of dependence.

**Negative evidence.** No audible rustling, crowd speech, running, or walking supports `root_000014/B001`. Mere intensity inherent in salinity does not meet the high burden for the single-source generalized maximum branch `B004`.
