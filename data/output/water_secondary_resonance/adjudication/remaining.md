## Strong findings

### Saturating purity at 76:21 (`root_000722/B009`)

The trigger is the same ayah's `ثِيَابُ سُندُسٍ خُضْرٌ وَإِسْتَبْرَقٌ`, which supplies both cloth and color immediately before `وَسَقَىٰهُمْ رَبُّهُمْ شَرَابًا طَهُورًا`. The dye branch contributes saturation: the drink is not merely served but images purity permeating the recipients as color permeates cloth. The exact material cue, adjacency, and concrete change make this distinctive from the ordinary afterlife drink baseline.

### Restored access at 28:24 (`root_000722/B002`)

At the Midian source, a crowd is already watering while two women hold back and explicitly cannot water until the shepherds depart. Moses' `فَسَقَىٰ لَهُمَا` therefore resolves an access barrier, not only a thirst or livestock task. The source-provision branch changes the verb into socially enabling use of a blocked communal resource, and the resulting invitation and recompense confirm the act's expanded function.

### The fully drawn image at 79:1 (`root_001080/B004`)

Unlike the drowning cohort, 79:1 has no overt water event. The governing `ٱلنَّٰزِعَٰتِ` supplies drawing or pulling, while `غَرْقًا` supplies the limit or utmost degree. Together they realize the root packet's bow drawn to full extension, giving the otherwise abstract intensive a precise mechanical image. This is an outlier-specific reading change, not a recycled judgment-drowning theme.

### Rendering heat at 22:19 (`root_000001/B003`)

Hot water is poured over heads and the next clause states `يُصْهَرُ بِهِۦ مَا فِى بُطُونِهِمْ وَٱلْجُلُودُ`. The rendering branch therefore names the liquid's exact operation on bodily material: `ٱلْحَمِيمُ` becomes an active solvent of interior and exterior, not merely a painful hot substance. The explicit instrumental pronoun keeps the mechanism word-local.

### Hot water joined to black smoke at 56:42 (`root_000001/B001`)

`فِى سَمُومٍ وَحَمِيمٍ` is followed immediately by `وَظِلٍّ مِّن يَحْمُومٍ`. The same-root realization of black smoke supplies a stronger trigger than general hell darkness, and the false shade makes the hot water one component of a dark, enveloping anti-refreshment environment. This concretely changes the target's color and atmospheric register.

### Bad rain against productive rain at 27:58 (`root_001431/B001`)

The final target `فَسَآءَ مَطَرُ ٱلْمُنذَرِينَ` evaluates the punitive shower; within the same window, 27:60 supplies sky-water that produces gardens. The ordinary rain branch is thus an explicit local counterfactual: this is not merely punishment that falls, but rain whose effect is the inverse of the productive pattern displayed beside it. The evaluative target bears the contrast most directly.

### Desired rain becoming punishment at 46:24 (`root_001431/B002`)

The people name the approaching cloud `عَارِضٌ مُّمْطِرُنَا`; the correction is immediate: it is what they hastened, a wind containing painful punishment that destroys everything. The punitive-rain branch operates inside the mistaken word itself, turning their desired provision label into tragic anticipation. The correction is exact, local, and distinctive from both actual rain and ordinary punitive-rain reports.

### Relief after despair at 42:28 (`root_001118/B004`)

`ٱلْغَيْثَ` comes explicitly `مِنۢ بَعْدِ مَا قَنَطُوا۟` and is coordinated with the spreading of mercy. These clauses provide both the distressed state and its reversal, so the relief branch changes the rain into providential release experienced by people, not merely delayed precipitation. The construction is more specific than the cohort's general provision baseline.

### Moral weight enacted by the downpour at 2:264 (`root_001619/B002`)

The window names injury and nullification, then shows the heavy rain stripping the rock and leaving the spenders unable to retain anything earned. The severity/weight branch makes the physical `وَابِلٌ` enact the harmful consequence of the ostentatious act: rainfall intensity becomes moral `وبال`. The controlled contrast with the fruitful garden in 2:265 confirms that the effect discloses the recipient rather than defining all heavy rain.

### Sight endangered by rain's display at 24:43 (`root_001636/B004`)

This route bears a high anomaly burden because the eye-disease usage is marked anomalous in the root packet. It passes because the trigger is unusually exact: the rain-bearing cloud display `يَكَادُ ... يَذْهَبُ بِٱلْأَبْصَٰرِ`, followed by another `أَبْصَار` cue. The target therefore participates in a display that solicits seeing while nearly removing sight; that precise paradox overcomes the branch's burden.

### Timed entry at Midian, 28:23 (`root_001640/B002`)

The assigned-turn branch makes arrival an encounter with a regulated share and delay rather than a geographic endpoint. That change attaches to `وَرَدَ` because entry into this schedule is exactly what determines the action that follows.

### Successive entry at Midian, 28:23 (`root_001640/B007`)

The watering crowd, the women positioned apart, and the paired movement of `ورود` and `صدور` display a resource used in sequence. The branch gives Moses' arrival the force of entering, and then altering, an ordered succession. It is independently supported from the assigned-share reading because it explains precedence and movement rather than entitlement.
