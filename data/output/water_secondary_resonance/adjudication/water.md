## Strong findings

### 2:74 — B008, dull heart-water

The target is ordinary water released by split stone. The secondary branch is triggered by `قُلُوبُكُم`, the repeated cognition frame (`تَعْقِلُونَ` / `عَقَلُوهُ`), and the comparison between stone and harder human hearts. B008 supplies the specialized image of excessive heart-water as dullness. The economical bridge is contrastive: stone's inner water issues outward in response, while informed hearts fail to issue an intelligent response. The water therefore becomes a counter-measure of responsiveness, a distinctive change not supplied by the ordinary emergence reading.

### 6:99 — B006, ripening freshness

The primary water is rain producing diverse growth. The focus ayah then directs attention to fruit at fruiting and specifically to `يَنْعِهِ`, alongside greenness, dates, grapes, olives, and pomegranates. B006 expressly includes fruit filling with water as it ripens and gains visible freshness. The causal production chain attaches that branch to the target: external rain is perceived again as the fruit's inward moisture, maturity, and inspectable sheen. This is narrower and more surprising than the cohort's generic rain-to-growth baseline.

### 10:24 — B005, gilded false surface

The primary target is rain in the worldly-life parable. The same ayah supplies `زُخْرُفَهَا`, `ٱزَّيَّنَتْ`, the owners' confidence that they control the flourishing scene, and its sudden erasure. B005 moves from metallic wash to deceptive surface presentation. Water is the mechanism that raises the world's attractive covering and thereby enables false confidence before the surface is stripped away. The result changes water from a growth input into the maker of a persuasive but unstable display.

### 13:17 — B005, surface sorted from substance

Rain fills valleys and carries raised foam. The ayah then introduces heated metal for ornament or utility, gives it corresponding foam, and explicitly names truth and falsehood. This is an unusually exact branch trigger: metalwork and ornament activate B005's coating domain, while the paired foams enact surface versus enduring substance. The water does not merely flow; it exposes and carries away the swollen false surface while benefit remains.

### 22:5 — B004, generative deposition

The target is literal rain reviving inert earth. Within the same ayah, `نُّطْفَةٍ`, successive embryonic stages, and `نُقِرُّ فِى ٱلْأَرْحَامِ` present male fluid and deliberate placement in wombs before the earth receives water, swells, and produces attractive growth. B004 supplies precisely the male-fluid-in-womb mechanism. The tight same-ayah parallel makes rain echo deposited generative fluid across the two revival sequences without replacing its literal sense.

### 24:39 — B005, apparent water as false presentation

The target is what a thirsty person takes the mirage to be. `كَسَرَابٍ`, `يَحْسَبُهُ ... مَآءً`, approach, and `لَمْ يَجِدْهُ شَيْـًٔا` form a complete presentation-belief-disconfirmation mechanism. B005 includes deceptive display derived from surface coating. Here the water word names the projected satisfying surface itself, so it contributes the precise false promise made by the unbelievers' works rather than a merely thematic association with deception.

### 77:20 — B003, deliberately emplaced fluid

The primary target is lowly reproductive fluid. The immediately anaphoric `فَجَعَلْنَٰهُ فِى قَرَارٍ مَّكِينٍ` makes that same fluid content deliberately put into and retained within a secure receptacle until a measured term. B003 includes putting water into something, and the construction directly attaches its transitive placement role to the target. The branch changes the reading from emitted biological material to administered, protected, and timed emplacement.

## Supported patterns

- **Ripening and transient freshness (B006):** B-grade cases at 10:24, 13:4, 18:45, 22:5, 23:18, 35:27, and 50:9 attach water to fruit maturation, visible freshness, or its collapse. They remain below A where moisture, sheen, or branch scope requires one inferential step.
- **Distinctive placement or administration (B003):** 11:44, 18:29, 23:18, 28:23, 47:15, and 54:28 support routed, stored, socially allocated, or punitively imposed water. These are stronger than ordinary supply because the local construction specifies an unusual recipient relation or delivery mechanism.
- **Generative fluid echoes (B004):** 15:22 and 25:54 support reproductive resonance through fertilization/life or lineage/affinity cues, but each leaves either the deposition bridge or secondary status less secure than the A-grade same-ayah womb construction at 22:5.
- **Withdrawal and restored access (B002/B003):** the second water token in 67:30 supports both renewed appearance and agent-dependent supply. The sharp hidden/access contrast is real, but it remains close to the token's primary qualification.

## Rejected or downgraded patterns

- **Primary rain behavior:** most B002 activations merely redescribe water descending, entering earth, emerging, producing growth, or becoming plentiful. This includes ordinary revival passages and explicit downpour or flood modifiers; B002 produces the largest share of primary-entailment rejections.
- **Ordinary supply:** many B003 activations restate direct watering, washing, drinking provision, agricultural delivery, or pouring. A named giver, beneficiary, or purpose does not alone create secondary resonance because source-to-access is already a major cohort schema.
- **Generic vegetation as B006:** fruit lists without ripening/color/sheen predicates, and broad greenness or garden beauty beyond the branch's stated face, speech, fruit, and livestock scope, are C rather than supported. The B006 proposal at 39:21 is also downgraded because fine speech and softened hearts attach only by analogy to the water token.
- **Remote reproductive and cognition cues:** 6:99 B004 and 32:8 B008 have relevant vocabulary but weak word-local attachment. The former depends on a contrast that excludes the reproductive mechanism from God; the latter treats generic ingratitude as the specialized dull-heart idiom.
- **Cross-token or reversed activation:** 11:43 B003, 24:45 B002, and the first token of 67:30 B002 have visible local cues, but the cue belongs to a later token, a different use in the window, or the opposite event. They remain exploratory rather than accepted.
- **Literal flood abundance:** 11:43-44, 54:11-12, and 69:11 are not upgraded merely because their quantities are dramatic. The protocol and cohort baseline identify overwhelming floodwater as an ordinary primary subcluster.
