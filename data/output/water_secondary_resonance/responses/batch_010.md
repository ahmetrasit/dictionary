# batch_010 Water Secondary Resonance

## 44:24 — وَٱتْرُكِ ٱلْبَحْرَ رَهْوًا إِنَّهُمْ جُندٌ مُّغْرَقُونَ

### بَحْرَ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is the water body that is to be left in its present condition so that the pursuing force can enter the scene of its drowning.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The repeated ترك reverses agency: Moses is told to leave the sea, and the doomed people then leave gardens, springs, crops, and an honored station. The sea is therefore not merely terrain; it is the hinge by which pursuers become dispossessed.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** رَهْوًا and the later عُيُونٍ, زُرُوعٍ, and مَقَامٍ alter the scene around the sea, but none directly activates an accepted non-water branch of ب ح ر strongly enough to change بَحْرَ itself.

**Negative evidence.** The open route might suggest `root_000086/B003` (wide cleft), and the lost estates might suggest `root_000086/B002` or `root_000086/B006`, but the window contains neither a lexical cleaving cue nor an explicit breadth or water-holding-ground construction. Those associations remain scene-level only.

### مُّغْرَقُونَ — غ ر ق

**Primary reading.** The passive causative participle presents the army as people destined to be made to drown.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The verdict precedes its narrated completion: while the army is still following, it is already grammatically classified by its end. The following inventory of what they left makes the drowning a transfer from armed pursuit to complete loss.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** جُندٌ supplies a collective patient and مُّتَّبَعُونَ supplies the pursuit sequence; these intensify the primary drowning prediction without independently cueing another غ ر ق branch.

**Negative evidence.** Collective destruction could loosely evoke `root_001080/B005` (occupying or absorbing a whole field), but neither an exhaustion, total-scope, gaze, breath, nor surpassing construction appears. `root_001080/B002` is not retained as secondary because killing by drowning is already contained in the target's ordinary causative contribution.

## 45:12 — ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِى سَخَّرَ لَكُمُ ٱلْبَحْرَ لِتَجْرِىَ ٱلْفُلْكُ فِيهِ بِأَمْرِهِۦ وَلِتَبْتَغُوا۟ مِن فَضْلِهِۦ وَلَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ

### بَحْرَ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is the water body made serviceable for people, specifically as the medium in which ships run and through which bounty is sought.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window places this usable sea between failed human acquisitions and allies in 45:10 and the subjection of everything in the heavens and earth in 45:13. Maritime gain is thus recast from autonomous earning into a commanded gift that calls for gratitude and reflection.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B009` (sea travel and relation) is retained. The branch gives the target an operational contribution: بَحْر is not only a body of water but the enabling domain of seafaring.

**Activation trace.** The explicit construction لِتَجْرِىَ ٱلْفُلْكُ فِيهِ is a visible travel cue, while لِتَبْتَغُوا۟ مِن فَضْلِهِ gives the voyage its purpose. Because ships are stated to move in the sea, the bridge to the accepted sea-travel branch is direct rather than thematic.

**Negative evidence.** جَمِيعًا and the heaven-earth span may make the scene feel expansive, but that is insufficient to retain `root_000086/B002`; no use of breadth in knowledge, wealth, speech, pasture, or running is assigned to the target itself. The other branches have no local trigger.

## 52:6 — وَٱلْبَحْرِ ٱلْمَسْجُورِ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The target names the sea as an oath-object, qualified by ٱلْمَسْجُورِ and placed in a compact sequence of solemn cosmic images.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The inhabited house, raised roof, and charged sea lead immediately to an unavoidable punishment. A familiar water body is made evidentiary: its extraordinary condition helps certify that what is announced as لَوَٰقِعٌ cannot be دفع.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** ٱلْمَسْجُورِ sharply transforms the sea's state, while لَوَٰقِعٌ and دَافِعٍ transform its rhetorical role. These are strong ayah-level cues but do not visibly instantiate another accepted ب ح ر branch.

**Negative evidence.** Neither saltiness (`root_000086/B004`), cleaving (`root_000086/B003`), nor non-water breadth (`root_000086/B002`) is named. Inferring one merely from an unusual sea-state would replace evidence with possibility.

## 55:19 — مَرَجَ ٱلْبَحْرَيْنِ يَلْتَقِيَانِ

### بَحْرَيْنِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The dual names two seas released so that they meet, while the next ayah states that a barrier between them prevents either from transgressing.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The paired seas echo the paired easts and wests, but they are also personified by meeting and not overstepping. Cosmic duality becomes regulated relation rather than undifferentiated merger.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B010` (unplanned encounter) is retained under its higher evidentiary burden. It overlays the two water bodies with the image of parties brought into encounter, making their meeting, not only their volume, part of what بَحْرَيْنِ contributes.

**Activation trace.** The immediate verb يَلْتَقِيَانِ is an exact visible encounter cue, and مَرَجَ presents the two as released into that meeting rather than as agents shown arranging it. The following بَيْنَهُمَا بَرْزَخٌ and لَّا يَبْغِيَانِ then define the encounter by a boundary. This unusually explicit meeting construction is sufficient to activate the single-source branch.

**Negative evidence.** The paired horizons do not by themselves license `root_000086/B002` (breadth), and the barrier does not say that either sea is cleft, so `root_000086/B003` is not retained.

## 55:24 — وَلَهُ ٱلْجَوَارِ ٱلْمُنشَـَٔاتُ فِى ٱلْبَحْرِ كَٱلْأَعْلَٰمِ

### بَحْرِ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The sea is the medium in which the constructed vessels travel, their scale rendered by comparison with conspicuous mountains or landmarks.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Pearls and coral emerge from the paired waters before towering ships appear on the sea; immediately afterward, everyone upon the earth is said to perish. Valuable natural yield and monumental craft are thus placed together under divine possession and against human finitude.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B009` (sea travel and relation) is retained. It makes the target contribute a navigable domain and a relation of maritime movement, not merely a location surrounding the ships.

**Activation trace.** ٱلْجَوَارِ is a moving-vessel designation, ٱلْمُنشَـَٔاتُ identifies the vessels as raised or constructed, and فِى ٱلْبَحْرِ locates their motion directly in the target. These cues visibly realize the sea-travel branch.

**Negative evidence.** كَٱلْأَعْلَٰمِ magnifies the ships, not the lexical breadth of بَحْر, so it does not independently activate `root_000086/B002`. No other branch has a local trigger.

## 81:6 — وَإِذَا ٱلْبِحَارُ سُجِّرَتْ

### بِحَارُ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The plural seas are presented as undergoing the catastrophic passive event سُجِّرَتْ within a chain of end-time reversals.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Prized she-camels are abandoned, wild animals are gathered, seas are transformed, souls are paired, and the buried girl is questioned. The sea's upheaval belongs to a sequence in which ordinary classifications and controls collapse so that moral accountability can come forward.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The repeated وَإِذَا plus passive verbs creates a tight sequence of imposed transformations. That sequence changes the seas' narrative role, but سُجِّرَتْ does not visibly cue saltiness, cleaving, travel, breadth, or another accepted ب ح ر branch.

**Negative evidence.** The plural form alone is not evidence for `root_000086/B002` (expanse), and catastrophic alteration alone does not establish `root_000086/B003` (wide cleft). No secondary branch passes the standard.

## 82:3 — وَإِذَا ٱلْبِحَارُ فُجِّرَتْ

### بِحَارُ — ب ح ر

**Primary reading.** The seas are water bodies made to burst open or break forth as part of the cosmic dissolution.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Sky, stars, seas, and graves undergo parallel disassembly before a self knows what it sent ahead and left behind. The marine rupture is therefore one scale in a sequence that moves from the cosmos to personal disclosure.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000086/B003` (wide cleft) is retained. It adds a boundary-breaking image to the water body: the seas contribute not only released water but the opening of what had contained or divided it.

**Activation trace.** The target's own passive فُجِّرَتْ is a direct rupture cue. It is reinforced locally by ٱنفَطَرَتْ for the sky, ٱنتَثَرَتْ for the stars, and بُعْثِرَتْ for the graves. The repeated opening and scattering supplies an abductive bridge from bursting seas to the accepted wide-cleft branch.

**Negative evidence.** No animal-ear or bahirah referent is imported from `root_000086/B003`; only its cleft image resonates. The window gives no cue for saltiness, travel, bewilderment, or the root's single-source bodily and medical branches.

## 2:60 — وَإِذِ ٱسْتَسْقَىٰ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِۦ فَقُلْنَا ٱضْرِب بِّعَصَاكَ ٱلْحَجَرَ فَٱنفَجَرَتْ مِنْهُ ٱثْنَتَا عَشْرَةَ عَيْنًا قَدْ عَلِمَ كُلُّ أُنَاسٍ مَّشْرَبَهُمْ كُلُوا۟ وَٱشْرَبُوا۟ مِن رِّزْقِ ٱللَّهِ وَلَا تَعْثَوْا۟ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ مُفْسِدِينَ

### ٱسْتَسْقَىٰ — س ق ي

**Primary reading.** Moses seeks water on behalf of his people; the following command and twelve issuing springs constitute the answer to that request.

**Ayah-level surprise.** A need for drink is answered through a struck stone with twelve differentiated springs, so relief arrives together with social order. The next ayah's dissatisfaction with one food places this abundant, apportioned answer against a renewed demand for variety.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000722/B006` (prayer for watering or rain) and `root_000722/B002` (providing a watering source) are retained. B006 makes the request intercessory address, while B002 makes its object durable access to sources rather than one immediate drink.

**Activation trace.** مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِ and the answering فَقُلْنَا visibly frame the request as prayer on others' behalf, activating B006. ٱثْنَتَا عَشْرَةَ عَيْنًا followed by كُلُّ أُنَاسٍ مَّشْرَبَهُمْ supplies persistent, group-differentiated water access, directly activating B002. The bridge is the movement from petition to provisioned infrastructure.

**Negative evidence.** The springs are for drinking, not explicitly irrigation, so `root_000722/B003` is not retained; the crop list in 2:61 belongs to a new demand and does not establish an irrigation share in the focus ayah. No cloud, bodily fluid, dye, or heart-forcing cue appears.

### مَّشْرَبَ — ش ر ب

**Primary reading.** مَّشْرَبَهُمْ denotes each group's recognized drinking place or source.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The singular possessive allotted to every group stands beside twelve springs and the universal كُلُّ أُنَاسٍ. A miraculous abundance is made legible as differentiated access, not an undirected mass of water.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000783/B002` (allotted share of water) and `root_000783/B014` (watered land or green growth) are retained. B002 adds an apportioned entitlement to the locative sense; B014 extends the drinking source toward the land's productive water-dependence visible in the next ayah.

**Activation trace.** The counted ٱثْنَتَا عَشْرَةَ عَيْنًا, distributive كُلُّ أُنَاسٍ, possessive مَّشْرَبَهُمْ, and cognition marker قَدْ عَلِمَ jointly activate B002. For B014, the immediate continuation مِمَّا تُنۢبِتُ ٱلْأَرْضُ followed by a concrete crop list is a visible green-growth cue; it lets the assigned drinking source resonate as part of the water-fed ecology from which food is requested.

**Negative evidence.** B014 supplements rather than converts مَّشْرَب into farmland; the target remains a drinking place. Drinkers as a social group (`root_000783/B003`) is not separately retained because كُلُّ أُنَاسٍ marks recipients, not a lexical co-drinking relation.

### ٱشْرَبُ — ش ر ب

**Primary reading.** The imperative commands the people to drink from God's provision alongside the command to eat.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Permission to consume is immediately bounded by a prohibition against فساد in the earth, and the next ayah shows desire turning from received provision toward substitution. Drinking is therefore presented as grateful use subject to conduct, not bare satisfaction.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000783/B002` (allotted share of water) is retained. It makes the act of drinking participation in an ordered allocation rather than indiscriminate access.

**Activation trace.** Immediately before the imperative, twelve springs are counted and every group is said to know مَّشْرَبَهُمْ. That explicit distributive arrangement supplies the branch cue; the bridge is that the commanded act draws from each group's known share.

**Negative evidence.** مِن رِّزْقِ ٱللَّهِ and the eat-drink pairing enrich the ethics of consumption but do not state the wealth-spending idiom required for `root_000783/B012`. No color absorption, rope, bodily-part, or understanding construction changes this literal imperative.

## 2:93 — وَإِذْ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَٰقَكُمْ وَرَفَعْنَا فَوْقَكُمُ ٱلطُّورَ خُذُوا۟ مَآ ءَاتَيْنَٰكُم بِقُوَّةٍ وَٱسْمَعُوا۟ قَالُوا۟ سَمِعْنَا وَعَصَيْنَا وَأُشْرِبُوا۟ فِى قُلُوبِهِمُ ٱلْعِجْلَ بِكُفْرِهِمْ قُلْ بِئْسَمَا يَأْمُرُكُم بِهِۦٓ إِيمَٰنُكُمْ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤْمِنِينَ

### أُشْرِبُ — ش ر ب

**Primary reading.** The passive causative depicts the calf as having been imbued into their hearts through disbelief: an attachment has permeated the inner seat of response.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The command to take revelation firmly and listen is answered by سَمِعْنَا وَعَصَيْنَا, after which the calf is described as what actually enters the heart. Hearing and inward uptake split apart: the rejected command remains external while the idol becomes internal.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000783/B011` (absorbing as understanding) and `root_000783/B008` (putting a rope around the neck) are retained. B011 supplies an ironic cognition contrast: they hear without truly taking in the command, yet take in the calf. B008 adds a tethering image in which the internalized calf becomes a binding attachment.

**Activation trace.** For B011, the exact sequence وَٱسْمَعُوا۟ / سَمِعْنَا / وَعَصَيْنَا visibly stages failed hear-and-understand uptake. For B008, ٱلْعِجْلَ is the explicit animal cue, while أَخَذْنَا مِيثَٰقَكُمْ, خُذُوا۟, and بِقُوَّةٍ create a repeated holding and binding frame. The bridge is not that the heart becomes a literal neck, but that calf-attachment functions like a tether under the branch's attested rope image.

**Negative evidence.** The primary permeation already corresponds to the physical and affective absorption image of `root_000783/B007`, so it is not counted as an additional secondary resonance. The accusation, neck-craning, vessel, and water-share branches have no visible local trigger.

## 2:187 — أُحِلَّ لَكُمْ لَيْلَةَ ٱلصِّيَامِ ٱلرَّفَثُ إِلَىٰ نِسَآئِكُمْ هُنَّ لِبَاسٌ لَّكُمْ وَأَنتُمْ لِبَاسٌ لَّهُنَّ عَلِمَ ٱللَّهُ أَنَّكُمْ كُنتُمْ تَخْتَانُونَ أَنفُسَكُمْ فَتَابَ عَلَيْكُمْ وَعَفَا عَنكُمْ فَٱلْـَٰٔنَ بَٰشِرُوهُنَّ وَٱبْتَغُوا۟ مَا كَتَبَ ٱللَّهُ لَكُمْ وَكُلُوا۟ وَٱشْرَبُوا۟ حَتَّىٰ يَتَبَيَّنَ لَكُمُ ٱلْخَيْطُ ٱلْأَبْيَضُ مِنَ ٱلْخَيْطِ ٱلْأَسْوَدِ مِنَ ٱلْفَجْرِ ثُمَّ أَتِمُّوا۟ ٱلصِّيَامَ إِلَى ٱلَّيْلِ وَلَا تُبَٰشِرُوهُنَّ وَأَنتُمْ عَٰكِفُونَ فِى ٱلْمَسَٰجِدِ تِلْكَ حُدُودُ ٱللَّهِ فَلَا تَقْرَبُوهَا كَذَٰلِكَ يُبَيِّنُ ٱللَّهُ ءَايَٰتِهِۦ لِلنَّاسِ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَّقُونَ

### ٱشْرَبُ — ش ر ب

**Primary reading.** The imperative permits drinking during the fasting night until dawn becomes distinguishable, after which the fast is completed until night.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The permission is embedded between divine nearness and response in 2:186, repentance within 2:187, and prohibitions on consuming wealth in 2:188. Bodily permission is therefore neither isolated indulgence nor mere scheduling; it belongs to a responsive, forgiven, and ethically bounded life.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000783/B002` (allotted share of water) and `root_000783/B012` (wealth consumed in feeding and watering) are retained. B002 turns the drink into a temporally allotted portion; B012 makes the lawful eat-drink pair resonate against the immediately following unlawful consumption of wealth.

**Activation trace.** For B002, حَتَّىٰ, the white/black thread contrast, dawn, ثُمَّ, night, and حُدُودُ ٱللَّهِ visibly measure the permitted turn for drinking; 2:185's عِدَّة and 2:189's مَوَٰقِيت reinforce that allotment frame. For B012, وَكُلُوا۟ وَٱشْرَبُوا۟ is followed immediately by وَلَا تَأْكُلُوٓا۟ أَمْوَٰلَكُم and a second لِتَأْكُلُوا۟ ... أَمْوَٰلِ ٱلنَّاسِ. That exact consumption-and-wealth sequence bridges the branch to the target, contrasting licensed nourishment with illicit resource consumption.

**Negative evidence.** The repeated بيان language governs recognition of limits but does not make ٱشْرَبُوا itself mean understanding, so `root_000783/B011` is not retained. No vessel, drinker-group, unpleasant-water, absorption, rope, or watered-land cue changes this target.
