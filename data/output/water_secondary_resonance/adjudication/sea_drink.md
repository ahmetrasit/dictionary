- **Social drinking organization:** B003 is supported at 54:28, the plural target in 2:249, 7:160, and both 26:155 shares. These windows visibly organize participants around common or alternating water access without always producing a surprising target change.
- **Aversive but compelled intake:** B005 is supported at 18:29, 56:68, 56:54, both 56:55 targets, and 78:24. Explicitly hostile quality, counterfactual salinization, thirst, or denied relief makes drinkability persist under aversion; the recurrent punishment cluster limits distinctiveness.

## Rejected or downgraded patterns

- **Ordinary sea travel was overactivated.** Fourteen of sixteen B009 candidates are rejected because ships, carriage, work, bounty, navigation, peril, or rescue are already primary cohort uses; 18:60 is only C because the journey reaches a sea-junction without depicting sea-riding.
- **Literal scale is not transferred breadth.** B002 is rejected at 6:59 and 24:40 because geographic totality and a deep layered sea remain properties of the water body excluded by the branch; 14:32 and 18:63 are C because wealth/knowledge expansion is only indirectly attached.
- **Splitting vocabulary must reach the target.** B003 at 6:97 is C because preceding `فَالِقُ` language creates a motif but does not open that sea; target-ayah clefts and corridors grade much higher.
- **Saltiness stated as water quality is primary.** B004 at 25:53 and 35:12 is rejected because `مِلْحٌ أُجَاجٌ` directly predicates the ordinary contrast rather than generating secondary surprise.
- **Remote keywords do not override branch boundaries.** B005 at 30:41 fails because generic manifestation is not the fixed exposed-encounter idiom; B010 at 55:19 fails because deliberately meeting seas are neither humans nor an unplanned encounter.
- **Cross-parable attachment remains exploratory.** B014 at 24:40 has an exact thirst cue in the preceding mirage but cannot securely transfer that unquenched thirst to the separate deep-sea target.
- **Negative quality frames are not active branches by default.** B005 is rejected at 16:66 and 37:46 because purity, ease, pleasure, and absence of harm negate unpleasant drinkability; it is also rejected at 47:15 because the bad water belongs to the contrasted group, not the target drinkers.
- **Generic reward is not a lexical share or wealth idiom.** B012 at 52:19 and B002 at 77:43 are rejected because bestowed recompense alone supplies neither consumed wealth nor a bounded water allotment.
- **Same-source growth can remain too remote from the drink word.** B007 and B014 at 16:10 and B014 at 2:60 are C: vegetation is visible, but uptake or watered land attaches more directly to the water-source ecology than to the target morpheme.
- **Contrastive restraints need a target-local mechanism.** B008 at 76:5 and 76:6 is C because nearby chains sharpen the fate contrast without making drinking itself a tether or untethering action; B007 at 76:6 is likewise C because affection-driven feeding is not securely carried by the spring-drinking target.
