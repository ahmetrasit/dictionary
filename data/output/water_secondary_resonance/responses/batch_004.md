# batch_004 Water Secondary Resonance

## 24:45 — وَٱللَّهُ خَلَقَ كُلَّ دَآبَّةٍ مِّن مَّآءٍ فَمِنْهُم مَّن يَمْشِى عَلَىٰ بَطْنِهِۦ وَمِنْهُم مَّن يَمْشِى عَلَىٰ رِجْلَيْنِ وَمِنْهُم مَّن يَمْشِى عَلَىٰٓ أَرْبَعٍ يَخْلُقُ ٱللَّهُ مَا يَشَآءُ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

### مَّآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Ordinary water is presented as the common created medium from which every moving creature originates, before the ayah differentiates creatures by their modes of walking.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window moves from the visible formation and descent of weather to an unseen commonality within animal creation, then to clear signs, guidance, and a disputed profession of faith. The creatures' bodily diversity thus becomes an epistemic sign continuous with the cloud process, not merely a zoological list.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` is retained. Its image of water appearing, emerging, and descending makes the target water a dynamically administered life-source rather than inert source material.

**Activation trace.** In 24:43, `يُزْجِى سَحَابًا`, `ٱلْوَدْقَ يَخْرُجُ مِنْ خِلَٰلِهِۦ`, and `يُنَزِّلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ` visibly stage gathering, emergence, and descent. Because `root_001458/B002` expressly covers water appearing and the sky pouring water, that external process supplies an abductive model for `مِّن مَّآءٍ`: the same governed movement that makes water appear in the world also stands behind water's generative contribution within living creatures.

**Negative evidence.** `root_001458/B004` is not retained: `خَلَقَ كُلَّ دَآبَّةٍ` is generative, but the window supplies no male, womb, lineage, or deposition cue specific enough to the branch. No local trigger supports watering/pouring, plating, sheen, crystal, or heart-dullness branches.

## 25:48 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَرْسَلَ ٱلرِّيَٰحَ بُشْرًۢا بَيْنَ يَدَىْ رَحْمَتِهِۦ وَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً طَهُورًا

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target denotes clean, purifying rainwater sent down from the sky.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding alternation of sleep and daytime rising is followed by water reviving a dead town; the life-cycle image then becomes a test of remembrance when the distributed gift meets widespread ingratitude.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` and `root_001458/B003` are retained. B002 presents the rain as water made to appear from above in abundance; B003 adds purposeful delivery to recipients. Together they make `مَآءً طَهُورًا` both a descending event and an administered provision.

**Activation trace.** `أَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ` directly activates the sky-pouring scope of `root_001458/B002`. In 25:49, `وَنُسْقِيَهُۥ ... أَنْعَٰمًا وَأَنَاسِىَّ كَثِيرًا` visibly turns the descended water into intentional giving, activating the watering/supplying image of `root_001458/B003`. The bridge is the explicit sequence descent, revival, and giving drink: the target's purity serves a directed economy of life.

**Negative evidence.** Revival of the town does not by itself supply the face, speech, fruit-ripening, or visible-sheen cue required for B006. Nothing locally activates reproductive fluid, plating, crystal, or heart-dullness.

## 25:54 — وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ مِنَ ٱلْمَآءِ بَشَرًا فَجَعَلَهُۥ نَسَبًا وَصِهْرًا وَكَانَ رَبُّكَ قَدِيرًا

### مَآءِ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Water is the generative material from which a human being is created.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding ayah holds two waters apart with a barrier, while the focus ayah turns water into a human and then joins humans through descent and marriage. The window therefore juxtaposes divinely maintained separation with divinely made relation.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B004` is retained. Its male reproductive-fluid image narrows the generic material sense without displacing it, so the target also contributes the bodily means through which `نَسَبًا وَصِهْرًا` become possible.

**Activation trace.** The local sequence `خَلَقَ مِنَ ٱلْمَآءِ بَشَرًا فَجَعَلَهُۥ نَسَبًا وَصِهْرًا` supplies creation, a human offspring-bearing subject, descent, and affinity. Those unusually concentrated kinship cues activate `root_001458/B004`; the abductive bridge is that reproductive fluid is the water-local mechanism connecting individual creation to extended lineage.

**Negative evidence.** The two seas in 25:53 sharpen the ayah-level separation/connection contrast but do not activate another branch of م و ه. No supplying verb, water-emergence construction, metallic coating, sheen, crystal, or heart-dullness cue passes the threshold.

## 27:60 — أَمَّنْ خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ وَأَنزَلَ لَكُم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَنۢبَتْنَا بِهِۦ حَدَآئِقَ ذَاتَ بَهْجَةٍ مَّا كَانَ لَكُمْ أَن تُنۢبِتُوا۟ شَجَرَهَآ أَءِلَٰهٌ مَّعَ ٱللَّهِ بَلْ هُمْ قَوْمٌ يَعْدِلُونَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is rainwater sent from the sky as the cause by which flourishing gardens grow.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The destructive rain of 27:58 is answered by life-bearing rain in 27:60. The latter's garden beauty is then made evidence in a repeated challenge to association, so water's effect is simultaneously ecological and argumentative.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` and `root_001458/B006` are retained. B002 adds the event of water appearing from the sky; B006 adds water-like freshness and visible beauty in vegetation. The target consequently produces not only plant life but the garden's manifest `بَهْجَةٍ`.

**Activation trace.** `أَنزَلَ ... مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً` directly activates the sky-pouring image of `root_001458/B002`. The result `حَدَآئِقَ ذَاتَ بَهْجَةٍ` together with `شَجَرَهَا` visibly activates the flourishing, moisture-borne freshness of `root_001458/B006`. The bridge is the explicit causal `بِهِۦ`: the water's descent becomes perceptible as sheen, freshness, and delight in what grows.

**Negative evidence.** `لَكُم` indicates benefit but is not an act of watering or pouring into a recipient precise enough for B003. No reproductive, plating, crystal, or heart-water cue occurs.

## 28:23 — وَلَمَّا وَرَدَ مَآءَ مَدْيَنَ وَجَدَ عَلَيْهِ أُمَّةً مِّنَ ٱلنَّاسِ يَسْقُونَ وَوَجَدَ مِن دُونِهِمُ ٱمْرَأَتَيْنِ تَذُودَانِ قَالَ مَا خَطْبُكُمَا قَالَتَا لَا نَسْقِى حَتَّىٰ يُصْدِرَ ٱلرِّعَآءُ وَأَبُونَا شَيْخٌ كَبِيرٌ

### وَرَدَ — و ر د

**Primary reading.** Moses reaches or comes to the water-place of Midian.

**Ayah-level surprise.** His fearful departure and request for guidance end at a crowded water source where arrival immediately becomes an encounter with unequal access; the guided road thus leads into an unannounced act of service and eventual safety.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001640/B002` and `root_001640/B007` are retained. B002 makes the arrival an encounter with a socially timed water-share; B007 makes it entry into an ordered succession in which some users precede others.

**Activation trace.** `لَا نَسْقِى حَتَّىٰ يُصْدِرَ ٱلرِّعَآءُ` visibly gives the women a delayed access time, activating the apportioned or timed watering image of `root_001640/B002`. The crowd `أُمَّةً ... يَسْقُونَ`, the women held `مِن دُونِهِم`, and the ورود/صدور sequence display precedence and successive entry, activating `root_001640/B007`. The bridge is that Moses does not merely arrive geographically: he enters a regulated human sequence at the resource, which determines the moral significance of what follows.

**Negative evidence.** Nothing marks a fever cycle, vein, rose color, or drooping length. The repeated schedule and precedence cues are specific; no other branch is retained from the broad fact of arrival alone.

### مَآءَ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target names the actual water source at Midian around which people and livestock gather.

**Ayah-level surprise.** A physical resource becomes the place where crowd power, the women's restraint, Moses' intervention, recompense, and rescue from fear are successively disclosed.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B003` is retained. The branch of deliberately supplying water changes the target from a mere destination into the medium of a concrete service performed for others.

**Activation trace.** The same window repeats `يَسْقُونَ`, `لَا نَسْقِى`, `فَسَقَىٰ لَهُمَا`, and `مَا سَقَيْتَ لَنَا`. These overt س ق ي acts visibly activate the watering/pouring role of `root_001458/B003`. The abductive bridge is enacted rather than thematic: the water's narrative contribution is realized through who can deliver it, who must wait, and who gives it on another's behalf.

**Negative evidence.** The crowd does not show the water itself appearing, seeping, entering, or becoming abundant, so B002 is not retained. There is no trigger for reproductive fluid, coating, sheen, crystal, or heart-water.

### يَسْقُ — س ق ي

**Primary reading.** The men are actively giving water to their animals at the source, the direct giving-to-drink sense represented by the root's primary branch.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The ordinary public act is framed by the two women's enforced distance and is then answered by Moses performing that same act specifically for them.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `أُمَّةً مِّنَ ٱلنَّاسِ يَسْقُونَ` supplies a direct agent, act, and implied livestock recipients. The later `فَسَقَىٰ لَهُمَا` repeats that direct action but does not introduce a different accepted س ق ي branch.

**Negative evidence.** Waiting until the shepherds depart concerns access to the source, not irrigation of land or crops under `root_000722/B003`, and the men do not create a permanent supply under B002. No bodily fluid, rain-cloud noun, dyeing, hostile heart-saturation, or prayer-for-rain cue appears.

### نَسْقِى — س ق ي

**Primary reading.** The women say that they do not water their animals until the shepherds withdraw; the verb remains direct giving-to-drink, here under negation and delay.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The negated act exposes constrained agency: their problem is not lack of knowledge or intention but their position behind the crowd and their elderly father, a condition reversed when Moses waters for them.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `لَا نَسْقِى حَتَّىٰ يُصْدِرَ ٱلرِّعَآءُ` and the answering `فَسَقَىٰ لَهُمَا` keep the same direct watering action while changing agent and beneficiary. The sequence adds social force to the primary sense but supplies no distinct lexical branch.

**Negative evidence.** The delayed turn does not make the target an irrigation share or channel under `root_000722/B003`, nor does it mean establishing available water under B002. Moses' later prayer concerns need and descended good, not a formula seeking rain under B006; the remaining minor branches lack local triggers.

## 29:63 — وَلَئِن سَأَلْتَهُم مَّن نَّزَّلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَحْيَا بِهِ ٱلْأَرْضَ مِنۢ بَعْدِ مَوْتِهَا لَيَقُولُنَّ ٱللَّهُ قُلِ ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يَعْقِلُونَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is rainwater whose descent restores dead earth to life.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The visible death-and-life cycle of land is immediately set beside the merely playful `ٱلْحَيَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنْيَا` and the emphatic true life, `ٱلْحَيَوَانُ`, of the next abode. Water therefore supplies an observable model for a distinction the speakers verbally concede but fail to reason through.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` is retained. The branch's appearance and sky-pouring image makes the target an arriving influx that changes the state of the earth, not simply a substance present on it.

**Activation trace.** `نَّزَّلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً` directly realizes the sky-descending scope of `root_001458/B002`, and `فَأَحْيَا بِهِ ٱلْأَرْضَ` displays the state-change produced by its arrival. The abductive bridge is the explicit descent-to-revival sequence: water contributes a perceptible transition that underwrites the window's larger life contrast.

**Negative evidence.** No one waters a recipient or pours water into a vessel, so B003 is not activated. Earthly revival without a face, speech, ripening fruit, or explicit sheen is insufficient for B006; the cognition failure `لَا يَعْقِلُونَ` lacks the heart-water construction needed for B008.

## 30:24 — وَمِنْ ءَايَٰتِهِۦ يُرِيكُمُ ٱلْبَرْقَ خَوْفًا وَطَمَعًا وَيُنَزِّلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَيُحْىِۦ بِهِ ٱلْأَرْضَ بَعْدَ مَوْتِهَآ إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَءَايَٰتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يَعْقِلُونَ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Rainwater descends from the sky and revives the earth after its death.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The target lies inside a sensory and cognitive sequence: differences are signs for knowers, nightly rest and seeking provision are signs for hearers, lightning is shown between fear and hope, and revived earth is a sign for reasoners. The following summons from the earth makes agricultural emergence anticipate human emergence.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` is retained. Its water-appearance and sky-pouring branch adds eventfulness to the target: the life-bearing water is made to arrive out of the feared and hoped-for sky.

**Activation trace.** `يُنَزِّلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً` directly activates the celestial descent covered by `root_001458/B002`; `ٱلْبَرْقَ خَوْفًا وَطَمَعًا` supplies the immediately visible precursor to that arrival, and `فَيُحْىِۦ بِهِ` states its concrete result. The bridge joins anticipation, appearance, and revival, so the target contributes the resolved gift within the fear/hope polarity.

**Negative evidence.** `يَعْقِلُونَ` is a general cognition cue, not the specific abundant-heart-water idiom required for B008. No watering act, reproductive context, ripening-fruit sheen, plating, or crystal image appears; no further branch is retained.

## 31:10 — خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ بِغَيْرِ عَمَدٍ تَرَوْنَهَا وَأَلْقَىٰ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ رَوَٰسِىَ أَن تَمِيدَ بِكُمْ وَبَثَّ فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ دَآبَّةٍ وَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ زَوْجٍ كَرِيمٍ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is rainwater sent down to cause the earth to grow every noble kind of vegetation.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The promised gardens of bliss in 31:8 are followed by a terrestrial scene in which water produces `كُلِّ زَوْجٍ كَرِيمٍ`; that visible creation then grounds the challenge to show what rival objects have created and leads into the command to give thanks.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` and `root_001458/B006` are retained. B002 makes the target a descending appearance of water; B006 adds the moisture-borne freshness and excellence displayed in the `زَوْجٍ كَرِيمٍ` that it produces.

**Activation trace.** `أَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً` directly triggers the sky-pouring scope of `root_001458/B002`. The causal continuation `فَأَنۢبَتْنَا ... مِن كُلِّ زَوْجٍ كَرِيمٍ` visibly joins moist growth to a term of fine quality, activating the plant-freshness image of `root_001458/B006`. The bridge is that the water's contribution culminates not in undifferentiated biomass but in flourishing kinds whose quality can be seen and answered with gratitude.

**Negative evidence.** There is no direct act of giving water to a person, animal, vessel, or specific tree for B003. No reproductive-fluid, coating, crystal, or heart-dullness trigger is present.

## 32:8 — ثُمَّ جَعَلَ نَسْلَهُۥ مِن سُلَٰلَةٍ مِّن مَّآءٍ مَّهِينٍ

### مَّآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** Here water denotes the lowly reproductive fluid from whose extracted portion human progeny is made; this is the direct contextual sense represented by `root_001458/B004`, not a secondary overlay.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window stages an ascent from clay, through lowly fluid, to formed humanity receiving spirit, hearing, sight, and hearts. The material smallness of the origin sharpens both the gift of those faculties and the rebuke `قَلِيلًا مَّا تَشْكُرُونَ`.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B008` is retained as a contrastive resonance. Its idiom of excessive heart-water as dullness makes the target's humble bodily fluid a foil for the inner faculty that can remain unresponsive despite being bestowed.

**Activation trace.** The next ayah explicitly names `ٱلْأَفْـِٔدَةَ`, supplying the heart locus required by `root_001458/B008`, and immediately qualifies the recipients with `قَلِيلًا مَّا تَشْكُرُونَ`, a visible failure of inward response. The abductive bridge is contrast: after the target water accounts for bodily descent, the heart-water idiom diagnoses possible dullness in the elevated human interior. This supplements, rather than replaces, the reproductive-fluid reading.

**Negative evidence.** B004 is primary here and is therefore not counted as secondary. `أَحْسَنَ كُلَّ شَىْءٍ خَلَقَهُۥ` is too general to activate the face, speech, or fruit sheen of B006; no water descent, watering, plating, or crystal cue appears.

## 32:27 — أَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا۟ أَنَّا نَسُوقُ ٱلْمَآءَ إِلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ ٱلْجُرُزِ فَنُخْرِجُ بِهِۦ زَرْعًا تَأْكُلُ مِنْهُ أَنْعَٰمُهُمْ وَأَنفُسُهُمْ أَفَلَا يُبْصِرُونَ

### مَآءَ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is literal water driven to barren land so that crops emerge for animals and people to eat.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window first asks hearers to learn from ruined generations and then asks viewers to see barren ground made productive. The demanded `ٱلْفَتْحُ` and the judgment answer that follow make this ordinary food cycle a nearby demonstration of decisive change and belated recognition.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` and `root_001458/B003` are retained. B002 casts the water as entering and becoming manifest in dry land; B003 casts it as intentionally conveyed into a recipient. The target thus contributes both state-changing arrival and directed supply.

**Activation trace.** `نَسُوقُ ٱلْمَآءَ إِلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ ٱلْجُرُزِ` visibly supplies movement into land, activating the entry/appearance image of `root_001458/B002`; the purposeful transitive `نَسُوقُ ... إِلَى` activates the supplying/putting-water-into image of `root_001458/B003`. `فَنُخْرِجُ بِهِۦ زَرْعًا` confirms the result. The abductive bridge is the directed route from giver to barren recipient: water is not merely present but conducted so that hidden productivity becomes visible nourishment.

**Negative evidence.** Although crops emerge, the window supplies no ripening fruit, watery sheen, or explicit beauty cue precise enough for B006. No reproductive, plating, crystal, or heart-water branch is locally triggered.
