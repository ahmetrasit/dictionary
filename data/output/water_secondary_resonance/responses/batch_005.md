# batch_005 Water Secondary Resonance

## 35:27 — أَلَمْ تَرَ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَخْرَجْنَا بِهِۦ ثَمَرَٰتٍ مُّخْتَلِفًا أَلْوَٰنُهَا وَمِنَ ٱلْجِبَالِ جُدَدٌۢ بِيضٌ وَحُمْرٌ مُّخْتَلِفٌ أَلْوَٰنُهَا وَغَرَابِيبُ سُودٌ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The word denotes ordinary rainwater sent down from the sky and made the means by which differently colored fruits are brought forth.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window moves from rejected messengers, clear proofs, and an illuminating book to visible differences of color in fruit, rock, people, and animals, and then to the learned who fear God. Rain is thus placed inside a larger movement from a divine sending to differentiated, readable signs; that movement enriches the ayah without turning the water into “revelation.”

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` (water appearing, entering, or becoming abundant) and `root_001458/B006` (watery freshness or ripening sheen) are retained. B002 makes the water a hidden input whose entry becomes manifest in produce; B006 makes its contribution include the inward freshness that surfaces as ripened color.

**Activation trace.** For B002, `أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَخْرَجْنَا بِهِۦ` supplies the descent-to-emergence sequence, and the bridge is that water received from above becomes visibly present through what it brings out. For B006, `ثَمَرَٰتٍ مُّخْتَلِفًا أَلْوَٰنُهَا` directly supplies fruit and visible ripening/color cues; the branch inventory specifically locates watery freshness in fruit. Together they shift the target from generic rain to a medium whose concealed entry becomes outward freshness and differentiation.

**Negative evidence.** The illumination and clear proofs do not establish mirror/crystal clarity (`root_001458/B007`), and the nearby denial does not establish deceptive plating (`root_001458/B005`). No cue supports seminal fluid or dull “heart-water.”

## 39:21 — أَلَمْ تَرَ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ أَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَسَلَكَهُۥ يَنَٰبِيعَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ثُمَّ يُخْرِجُ بِهِۦ زَرْعًا مُّخْتَلِفًا أَلْوَٰنُهُۥ ثُمَّ يَهِيجُ فَتَرَىٰهُ مُصْفَرًّا ثُمَّ يَجْعَلُهُۥ حُطَٰمًا إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَذِكْرَىٰ لِأُو۟لِى ٱلْأَلْبَٰبِ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The word is ordinary rainwater: God sends it down, conducts it into springs in the earth, and uses it to produce vegetation that later dries and breaks apart.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The plant’s movement from color to yellow wreckage is called a reminder, then the window contrasts hard hearts with skins and hearts that soften toward God’s reminder and the best discourse. The material cycle therefore becomes a diagnostic image of receptivity, hardness, softening, and remembrance.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` and `root_001458/B006` are retained. B002 adds subterranean entry and reappearance to the target. B006 adds a carefully bounded resonance of attractive speech and renewed pliancy, so the water’s softening work in the physical sequence helps frame the later response to discourse.

**Activation trace.** B002 is directly triggered by `فَسَلَكَهُۥ يَنَٰبِيعَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ`: water is inserted into the earth and reappears as springs, exactly the branch’s entry/appearance image. B006 is triggered by `أَحْسَنَ ٱلْحَدِيثِ` together with `تَلِينُ جُلُودُهُمْ وَقُلُوبُهُمْ`; the branch includes sweetness of speech and watery freshness, and the abductive bridge is that the water-softened growth cycle supplies a physical analogue for discourse making receptive bodies and hearts pliant. This supplements, rather than replaces, the rainwater reading.

**Negative evidence.** `قَاسِيَةِ قُلُوبُهُم` is hardness, not the dullness idiom of abundant heart-water in `root_001458/B008`. No visible coating, crystal/mirror, or reproductive cue supports B005, B007, or B004.

## 41:39 — وَمِنْ ءَايَٰتِهِۦٓ أَنَّكَ تَرَى ٱلْأَرْضَ خَٰشِعَةً فَإِذَآ أَنزَلْنَا عَلَيْهَا ٱلْمَآءَ ٱهْتَزَّتْ وَرَبَتْ إِنَّ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَحْيَاهَا لَمُحْىِ ٱلْمَوْتَىٰٓ إِنَّهُۥ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

### مَآءَ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is literal water sent onto a still, humbled earth, after which the earth moves, swells, and lives; this is offered as evidence for reviving the dead.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The surrounding commands oppose worship of created heavenly signs to worship of their Creator, while the earth itself passes from `خَٰشِعَةً` to vigorous motion. Humility is therefore not lifelessness: under God’s act it becomes the posture from which responsive life appears.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B003` (supplying water by watering or pouring) is retained. It makes the noun contribute not only a reviving substance but an intentionally delivered supply directed to a recipient.

**Activation trace.** The construction `أَنزَلْنَا عَلَيْهَا ٱلْمَآءَ` visibly directs water onto the earth, and `أَحْيَاهَا` states the result. The bridge to B003 is the transitive recipient pattern: the earth is supplied with water and responds. The branch thus sharpens the target as administered life-provision within the resurrection proof.

**Negative evidence.** The ayah does not show seepage, a well, a ship, or marked abundance sufficient for B002. Motion and swelling are not fruit-ripening or facial/speech sheen, so B006 is not retained; the remaining branches lack local triggers.

## 43:11 — وَٱلَّذِى نَزَّلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءًۢ بِقَدَرٍ فَأَنشَرْنَا بِهِۦ بَلْدَةً مَّيْتًا كَذَٰلِكَ تُخْرَجُونَ

### مَآءًۢ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** This is literal rainwater sent from the sky in a measured amount, by which a dead land is revived as an analogy for human emergence.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The window joins an earth made a cradle with paths, measured rain, revived land, and conveyances made rideable. Resurrection is embedded in a sequence of proportioned provision, traversable routes, and controlled movement rather than presented as an isolated marvel.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B003` is retained. The measured descent and its directed reviving result activate supplying or pouring, making the target an apportioned provision rather than undifferentiated rainfall.

**Activation trace.** `نَزَّلَ ... مَآءًۢ بِقَدَرٍ` supplies the deliberate delivery and measure, while `فَأَنشَرْنَا بِهِۦ بَلْدَةً مَّيْتًا` supplies the recipient and effect. The bridge is that B003’s transitive supplying image is locally realized as a measured dose assigned to dead land. The target consequently bears the added force of calibrated life-provision.

**Negative evidence.** `بِقَدَرٍ` resists an inference of sheer abundance under B002, and the window contains no fruit-ripening sheen for B006, no crystal/mirror clarity, and no cue for B004, B005, or B008.

## 47:15 — مَّثَلُ ٱلْجَنَّةِ ٱلَّتِى وُعِدَ ٱلْمُتَّقُونَ فِيهَآ أَنْهَٰرٌ مِّن مَّآءٍ غَيْرِ ءَاسِنٍ وَأَنْهَٰرٌ مِّن لَّبَنٍ لَّمْ يَتَغَيَّرْ طَعْمُهُۥ وَأَنْهَٰرٌ مِّنْ خَمْرٍ لَّذَّةٍ لِّلشَّٰرِبِينَ وَأَنْهَٰرٌ مِّنْ عَسَلٍ مُّصَفًّى وَلَهُمْ فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ وَمَغْفِرَةٌ مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ كَمَنْ هُوَ خَٰلِدٌ فِى ٱلنَّارِ وَسُقُوا۟ مَآءً حَمِيمًا فَقَطَّعَ أَمْعَآءَهُمْ

### مَّآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The first water target denotes the water composing one of the promised garden’s rivers, explicitly characterized as not becoming stale or altered.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The four garden rivers are distinguished by preserved or desirable sensory qualities, then set against hot water that destroys inwardly. This contrast follows the opposition between clear evidence and evil made attractive, so the ayah tests apparent and actual quality without making the river water itself a metaphor.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The local work is fully carried by ordinary water plus the explicit modifier `غَيْرِ ءَاسِنٍ`. Neither the river construction nor the contrast with the later hot water visibly instantiates another branch of م و ه strongly enough to alter this first occurrence.

**Negative evidence.** `غَيْرِ ءَاسِنٍ` states absence of staleness, not mirror/crystal identity or optical clarity, so `root_001458/B007` is not retained. The later `سُقُوا۟ مَآءً حَمِيمًا` supplies B003 only for the separately administered second water target; importing it into this first river-water occurrence would erase the ayah’s contrast.

### ءَاسِنٍ — ء س ن

**Primary reading.** Within the negation `غَيْرِ ءَاسِنٍ`, the adjective contributes the rejected property of water becoming stale, foul, or altered; the river water never has that quality.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Its negated alteration participates in a larger contrast between garden liquids whose good qualities remain intact and the destructive hot water of the fire, while the surrounding ayat contrast clear evidence with desires that distort judgment.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `مَّآءٍ غَيْرِ ءَاسِنٍ` directly fixes the target in the ordinary stale-water branch. No other ء س ن image is locally named or enacted.

**Negative evidence.** There is no well air, dizziness, fainting, or death from fumes to activate `root_000033/B002`. No ropes, paternal likeness, kick, or named game supports B005-B008; the later intestinal cutting is not evidence for any of them.

### شَّٰرِبِينَ — ش ر ب

**Primary reading.** The plural names the drinkers for whom the wine river is a delight.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The next ayah presents listeners who hear the Prophet but immediately ask what he just said, followed by sealed hearts. Enjoyable drinking is thus placed beside failed reception: bodily intake and meaningful uptake sharply diverge.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000783/B005` (drinkable but unpleasant water) and `root_000783/B011` (absorbing as understanding) are retained. B005 turns the garden drinkers into one pole of a sensory contrast with recipients made to drink hot water. B011 adds a reception contrast: these drinkers truly take in delight, whereas the nearby listeners do not take in speech with understanding.

**Activation trace.** B005 is triggered by `سُقُوا۟ مَآءً حَمِيمًا فَقَطَّعَ أَمْعَآءَهُمْ`, an explicit later ingestion of maximally unpleasant water; the bridge is the opposed quality of what each group receives. B011 is triggered by `يَسْتَمِعُ إِلَيْكَ` followed by `مَاذَا قَالَ ءَانِفًا`, a visible hearing-without-grasping sequence; since B011 lexicalizes drinking as understanding, it makes `لِّلشَّٰرِبِينَ` resonate with successful uptake, in contrast to sealed-hearted listening.

**Negative evidence.** The rivers do not assign scheduled water shares, so B002 is not activated for this target. There is no rope fastening, neck craning, false accusation, waterskin treatment, or watered-land expression to support B008-B010 or B013-B014.

### سُقُ — س ق ي

**Primary reading.** The passive verb contributes the compelled administration of hot water to the people of the fire.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The same ayah that offers multiple delightful rivers reverses water-giving into punishment: delivery remains, but benefit becomes inward destruction.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** The passive construction `سُقُوا۟ مَآءً حَمِيمًا` and its result `فَقَطَّعَ أَمْعَآءَهُمْ` fully instantiate direct giving-to-drink, the target’s primary contribution. They do not independently instantiate another س ق ي branch.

**Negative evidence.** No enduring water source or irrigation share supports B002-B003, no prayer or rain cloud supports B006-B007, and the sealed hearts in the following ayah do not by themselves show the repeated hostile speech required for `root_000722/B010`.

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The second water target denotes literal scalding water administered to the people of the fire and cutting their intestines.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Water, normally associated in this ayah with the garden’s stable river, is retained as the same ordinary substance-category but given an opposite temperature, mode of delivery, and bodily effect.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B003` is retained. It makes this occurrence not merely hot liquid but water intentionally supplied to a recipient in a punitive act.

**Activation trace.** The immediately governing passive verb `سُقُوا۟` (س ق ي, direct giving to drink) is an exact visible trigger for B003’s supplying-water image, and `فَقَطَّعَ أَمْعَآءَهُمْ` fixes the consequence. The bridge is grammatical and causal: this water reaches the recipients by administered watering. The branch changes the noun’s contribution from substance alone to an imposed delivery-event.

**Negative evidence.** Heat and intestinal damage do not show water appearing in a well or entering a ship, so B002 is not retained. No fruit sheen, crystal clarity, deceptive coating, reproductive fluid, or dull-heart idiom is locally instantiated.

### حَمِيمًا — ح م م

**Primary reading.** The adjective specifies intensely hot or scalding water; its heat explains the destructive bodily result.

**Ayah-level surprise.** It converts the shared category “water” from an unaltered garden good into an instrument of torment, while the recipients’ immortality in fire prevents the damage from resolving into ordinary death.

**Water-root resonance.** No secondary water-root branch retained.

**Activation trace.** `مَآءً حَمِيمًا` directly selects the hot-water branch, and `فَقَطَّعَ أَمْعَآءَهُمْ` supplies its concrete effect. No additional ح م م image is required to explain the target.

**Negative evidence.** `ٱلنَّارِ` alone does not visibly provide charcoal, soot, or blackness for `root_000001/B001`. Intestinal destruction does not establish a decree or approaching death under B010, especially since the subject is `خَٰلِدٌ فِى ٱلنَّارِ`; no kinship, bathhouse, fever, sweat, sound, or aiming cue supports the other branches.

## 50:9 — وَنَزَّلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً مُّبَٰرَكًا فَأَنۢبَتْنَا بِهِۦ جَنَّٰتٍ وَحَبَّ ٱلْحَصِيدِ

### مَآءً — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is blessed rainwater sent from the sky and used to grow gardens and harvestable grain.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The preceding `تَبْصِرَةً وَذِكْرَىٰ` and the following `كَذَٰلِكَ ٱلْخُرُوجُ` make the rain-grown provision a visible mnemonic of resurrection. Its everyday production of food becomes instruction for an otherwise unseen return.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B006` is retained. It adds the water-like freshness and ripening moisture that becomes visible in attractive growth and date produce.

**Activation trace.** The window gives `بَهِيجٍ`, then growth `بِهِۦ`, and specifically `ٱلنَّخْلَ ... لَّهَا طَلْعٌ نَّضِيدٌ`. B006 explicitly includes freshness and moisture filling date fruit as it ripens. The bridge is that the blessed water’s inward presence becomes the visible freshness and ordered ripening of the palms; the target therefore contributes manifested vitality, not rainfall alone.

**Negative evidence.** Descent from the sky without an explicit abundance, seepage, well, or entry image is insufficient for B002 here. The growth effect does not add a separate pouring/supplying event beyond the primary rain construction, and no cue supports coating, crystal, seminal fluid, or heart-water.

## 54:11 — فَفَتَحْنَآ أَبْوَٰبَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ بِمَآءٍ مُّنْهَمِرٍ

### مَآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The word denotes literal rainwater pouring forcefully through the opened “doors” of the sky.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The torrent answers the preceding plea for victory, then water bursts from the earth, meets the descending water according to a decreed command, and bears the vessel. What looks like weather is narrated as a coordinated answer and judgment.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` is retained. It contributes the branch’s marked image of the sky pouring much water, turning generic rain into overwhelming appearance and abundance.

**Activation trace.** `فَفَتَحْنَآ أَبْوَٰبَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ` supplies an opening, `بِمَآءٍ مُّنْهَمِرٍ` supplies forceful continuous pouring, and the next ayah’s `فَٱلْتَقَى ٱلْمَآءُ` supplies accumulated convergence. These are direct local triggers for B002. The target consequently carries torrential emergence as an instrument coordinated with the earth’s waters.

**Negative evidence.** The vessel is carried on water; the text does not say water entered it, and no deliberate act of giving someone water activates B003. No fruit, sheen, crystal, plating, reproductive, or heart-water cue is present.

## 54:12 — وَفَجَّرْنَا ٱلْأَرْضَ عُيُونًا فَٱلْتَقَى ٱلْمَآءُ عَلَىٰٓ أَمْرٍ قَدْ قُدِرَ

### مَآءُ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The singular noun gathers the waters released from the earth and pouring from the sky; they meet in fulfillment of a decreed command.

**Ayah-level surprise.** Sky “doors,” earth “eyes,” converging water, and a vessel moving under divine eyes make the flood a system of opened channels, watched rescue, and commanded convergence rather than an undirected mass.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B002` is retained. It adds water’s appearance from the land and its massive accumulation to the collective singular target.

**Activation trace.** The prior `بِمَآءٍ مُّنْهَمِرٍ`, the current `فَجَّرْنَا ٱلْأَرْضَ عُيُونًا`, and `فَٱلْتَقَى ٱلْمَآءُ` form a visible sky-pouring/ground-emergence/convergence sequence. B002 covers both abundant sky-water and water appearing from land. The branch changes `ٱلْمَآءُ` from a generic collective into coordinated abundance emerging through distinct channels.

**Negative evidence.** `وَحَمَلْنَٰهُ عَلَىٰ ذَاتِ أَلْوَٰحٍ` shows carrying on a craft, not water entering it. No recipient is intentionally watered for B003, and the remaining branches lack local triggers.

## 54:28 — وَنَبِّئْهُمْ أَنَّ ٱلْمَآءَ قِسْمَةٌۢ بَيْنَهُمْ كُلُّ شِرْبٍ مُّحْتَضَرٌ

### مَآءَ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is literal water made the object of a division between the community and the she-camel, with each drinking turn attended at its time.

**Ayah-level surprise.** A basic shared resource becomes the structure of a test: announcement, allocation, attendance, breach, and punishment follow in tight sequence. Water organizes public obligation before the violence exposes refusal of that order.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B003` is retained. It adds the image of intentionally supplying water to recipients, here through an explicit allocation rather than an undirected natural source.

**Activation trace.** `ٱلْمَآءَ قِسْمَةٌۢ بَيْنَهُمْ` makes water an assigned provision, while `كُلُّ شِرْبٍ مُّحْتَضَرٌ` gives each supply-turn a recipient-facing occasion. The bridge is that division operationalizes the act covered by B003: water is deliberately made available to each side. The target therefore contributes administered social provision as well as physical water.

**Negative evidence.** No well, seepage, sky-pouring, fruit, sheen, mirror, coating, reproductive fluid, or dull-heart idiom supports B002 or B004-B008. The water’s division does not itself indicate abundance.

### شِرْبٍ — ش ر ب

**Primary reading.** The noun denotes an allotted share or scheduled turn of water, not merely the act of drinking.

**Ayah-level surprise.** `كُلُّ ... مُّحْتَضَرٌ` makes each share a publicly attended appointment. The drinking allocation is therefore both resource distribution and an observable test of compliance.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_000783/B003` (drinkers and co-drinkers) is retained. It adds the social relation around the share: each allotted turn identifies parties who attend and water in relation to one another.

**Activation trace.** `قِسْمَةٌۢ بَيْنَهُمْ` provides the between-parties construction, and `كُلُّ شِرْبٍ مُّحْتَضَرٌ` provides collective attendance at each turn. Since B003 includes co-drinkers and watering companions, the bridge is that an attended share exists as a socially coordinated drinking occasion. This changes the target from an abstract quota into a quota enacted among present participants.

**Negative evidence.** Water makes drinking implicit, but there is no separately narrated act of imbibing sufficient to retain B001 as a secondary reading. No vessel/place, unpleasant-water quality, permeation, rope, neck extension, understanding, accusation, waterskin treatment, or watered land is visible.

## 56:31 — وَمَآءٍ مَّسْكُوبٍ

### مَآءٍ — م و ه

**Primary reading.** The target is literal water characterized as poured out and available among the garden’s provisions.

**Ayah-level surprise.** The following fruit is abundant, neither cut off nor withheld. That negative pair makes the poured water part of an environment defined not only by plenty but by uninterrupted and unobstructed access.

**Water-root resonance.** `root_001458/B003` is retained. Its pouring/supplying image turns the target from a static quantity into provision continuously made available.

**Activation trace.** The passive participle `مَّسْكُوبٍ` is an exact pouring cue for B003, while `لَّا مَقْطُوعَةٍ وَلَا مَمْنُوعَةٍ` supplies the local image of supply without interruption or withholding. The bridge is that poured water is water presented for use; the branch adds active availability to the noun’s ordinary substance reading.

**Negative evidence.** `كَثِيرَةٍ` modifies fruit, not the water, so it does not independently establish B002’s water-abundance branch. Fruit is named but no ripening, moisture, color, or sheen activates B006. The other branches have no visible local triggers.
